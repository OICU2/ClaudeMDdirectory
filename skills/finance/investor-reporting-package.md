# Investor Reporting Package

Generates a complete, standardized investor update document for monthly or quarterly reporting periods, structured for clarity and professional credibility.

## Reporting Workflow

1. **Identify the reporting period** — confirm whether monthly or quarterly, and the specific period (e.g., Q3 2024, October 2024).

2. **Collect inputs** — request or extract the following from the user:
   - Revenue (actual vs. prior period vs. budget/forecast)
   - Gross margin and operating expenses
   - Cash position, burn rate, and runway
   - Headcount
   - 3–6 key business KPIs (e.g., ARR, MRR, churn, CAC, LTV, units sold, DAU)
   - Major milestones achieved this period
   - Challenges or risks
   - Forward-looking priorities or guidance

3. **Calculate variances** — for each financial line and KPI, compute:
   - Period-over-period change (absolute and %)
   - Actual vs. budget/forecast delta where data is provided
   - Flag items >10% variance for commentary

4. **Write variance commentary** — for each flagged item, produce a 1–2 sentence explanation covering: what drove the variance, whether it is structural or one-time, and any corrective action planned.

5. **Draft the narrative sections**:
   - Executive summary (3–5 sentences covering the period's headline story)
   - Wins and milestones (bullet list)
   - Challenges and risks (bullet list with mitigation notes)
   - Outlook and priorities for next period (3–5 bullets)

6. **Assemble in standard order** — follow the output structure below precisely.

7. **Tone calibration** — use direct, confident language. Acknowledge misses honestly. Avoid spin. Investors value accuracy over optimism.
