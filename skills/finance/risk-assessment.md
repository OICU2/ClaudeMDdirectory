# Financial Risk Assessment

This skill systematically identifies and quantifies financial risks across key categories and produces a structured risk register with severity ratings and mitigation strategies.

## Risk Assessment Workflow

1. **Scope the assessment** — Clarify the entity (company, project, portfolio, deal) and time horizon (short-term operational, medium-term strategic, long-term systemic).

2. **Identify risks across six core categories:**
   - **Market risk** — interest rate exposure, FX volatility, commodity price swings, equity market correlation
   - **Credit risk** — counterparty default, customer concentration, accounts receivable aging, debt covenants
   - **Liquidity risk** — cash runway, working capital gaps, debt maturity walls, access to credit facilities
   - **Operational risk** — key-person dependency, supply chain fragility, regulatory compliance gaps, fraud exposure
   - **Strategic risk** — competitive disruption, customer churn, margin compression, M&A integration failure
   - **Macroeconomic risk** — recession sensitivity, inflation pass-through, policy/regulatory changes, geopolitical factors

3. **Quantify each risk** — Assign likelihood (Low/Medium/High) and financial impact (estimated dollar range or % of revenue/EBITDA). Use available data; flag assumptions explicitly.

4. **Prioritize by expected impact** — Multiply likelihood score (1–3) × impact score (1–3) to produce a risk priority score (1–9). Focus depth of analysis on scores ≥ 6.

5. **Define mitigation actions** — For each high-priority risk, provide one or two concrete, actionable mitigation steps (e.g., hedging strategy, covenant renegotiation, supplier diversification).

6. **Flag data gaps** — Note any risks that cannot be quantified without additional information and specify exactly what data is needed.

## Output Format

Produce a structured risk register with the following sections:

**Executive Summary** (3–5 sentences): Top three risks, aggregate exposure estimate, and overall risk posture (Low / Moderate / High / Critical).

**Risk Register Table:**

| Risk | Category | Likelihood | Impact | Priority Score | Est. Exposure | Mitigation |
|------|----------|------------|--------|----------------|---------------|------------|
| [Risk name] | [Category] | L/M/H | L/M/H | 1–9 | $X or X% | [1–2 actions] |

Include 8–15 risks depending on complexity of the subject.

**Deep Dives** (for any risk scoring ≥ 6): One paragraph per high-priority risk covering root cause, trigger conditions, early warning indicators, and specific mitigation playbook.

**Data Gaps & Assumptions**: Bulleted list of what was assumed and what additional data would sharpen the analysis.

Total length: 600–1,200 words depending on complexity. Use tables and headers. No narrative filler.
