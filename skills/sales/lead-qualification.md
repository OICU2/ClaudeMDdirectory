# Lead Qualification Skill

Assesses incoming prospects against ideal customer profile (ICP) criteria to determine whether they merit further sales investment, producing a clear qualify/disqualify recommendation with reasoning.

## Qualification Framework

**Step 1: Gather Prospect Information**
Ask for or extract the following data points if not already provided:
- Company size (headcount and/or revenue)
- Industry and vertical
- Geography / market
- Current tech stack or tools in use
- Decision-maker role and seniority
- Budget signals (stated budget, funding stage, or spending indicators)
- Timeline to purchase
- Pain point or triggering event that brought them forward

**Step 2: Apply BANT + ICP Scoring**
Score the prospect across six dimensions (1–3 each):

| Dimension | 1 — Poor Fit | 2 — Partial Fit | 3 — Strong Fit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Budget | No budget / unknown | Budget exists but unclear | Confirmed budget aligned to price |
| Authority | End user only | Influencer present | Economic buyer engaged |
| Need | Vague interest | Acknowledged pain | Active problem seeking solution |
| Timeline | 12+ months or undefined | 3–12 months | Within 90 days |
| Company Profile | Outside ICP (size/industry) | Adjacent to ICP | Core ICP match |
| Competitive Position | Evaluating 5+ vendors | 2–4 vendors | Sole vendor or preferred |

**Step 3: Calculate Qualification Tier**
- **15–18 points → Tier 1: Hot Lead** — Prioritize immediately, fast-track to demo or proposal
- **10–14 points → Tier 2: Warm Lead** — Nurture with targeted follow-up, clarify gaps before advancing
- **6–9 points → Tier 3: Cold Lead** — Add to long-term nurture sequence, do not invest active sales time
- **Below 6 points → Disqualify** — Politely decline or refer elsewhere

**Step 4: Identify Blockers and Next Actions**
- Flag any single dimension scoring a 1 as a potential deal-killer requiring immediate clarification
- Recommend one concrete next action (e.g., discovery call, send case study, request budget conversation)
- Note any missing information that would change the score if obtained

## Output Format

Produce a structured qualification report in this format:

**Lead Qualification Report**

**Prospect:** [Company name / contact name]
**Date:** [Today's date]

**ICP Match Summary**
- Company Profile: [Brief description vs. ICP]
- Key Signals: [2–3 bullet points of strongest evidence]

**BANT + ICP Scorecard**
| Dimension | Score | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Budget | X/3 | [One sentence] |
| Authority | X/3 | [One sentence] |
| Need | X/3 | [One sentence] |
| Timeline | X/3 | [One sentence] |
| Company Profile | X/3 | [One sentence] |
| Competitive Position | X/3 | [One sentence] |

**Total Score:** X/18
**Qualification Tier:** [Tier 1 / 2 / 3 / Disqualify]

**Recommended Next Action:** [One specific, time-bound action with owner]
---

Keep the report concise — no more than one page. Use plain language; avoid jargon unless the user's team already uses it.
