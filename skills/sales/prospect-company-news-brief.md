# Prospect Company News Brief

Generates a structured intelligence brief on a target company by surfacing recent news, funding rounds, leadership changes, product launches, and other signal events that create timely, relevant outreach angles for sales reps.

## Research Workflow

1. **Identify the target**: Confirm company name, industry, and any known contacts or accounts. Ask if not provided.
2. **Pull news signals across these categories** (last 90 days unless otherwise specified):
   - Funding & financial events (Series rounds, IPO filings, M&A activity, layoffs, cost-cutting)
   - Executive changes (new C-suite hires, departures, promotions, board additions)
   - Product launches or major feature announcements
   - Partnerships, integrations, or new market expansions
   - Awards, rankings, press coverage, or analyst mentions
   - Regulatory actions, compliance news, or legal filings
   - Job postings that signal strategic priorities (e.g., hiring 10 sales engineers = scaling GTM)
3. **Score each signal by outreach relevance**:
   - High: Triggers a known pain point your product solves (e.g., new CTO often re-evaluates vendor stack)
   - Medium: Creates a natural conversation opener but no direct pain link
   - Low: Interesting context but weak outreach angle
4. **Draft outreach angles**: For each High and Medium signal, write one specific outreach hook — a single sentence connecting the news to a business outcome your product addresses.
5. **Flag timing urgency**: Note if any signal is time-sensitive (e.g., just closed funding = budget available now, exec just started = in first 90 days).

## Output Format

Produce a brief with these exact sections:

**Company:** [Name] | **Industry:** [Sector] | **Brief Date:** [Date] | **Timeframe Covered:** [e.g., Last 90 days]

---

### 🔥 Top Outreach Signals
Bulleted list of 2–4 highest-relevance signals, each formatted as:
- **[Signal type]:** [What happened] → **Angle:** [One sentence connecting it to a sales hook]

---

### 📰 Full News Summary
Grouped by category (Funding, Leadership, Product, Partnerships, Other). 2–4 bullets per category, skipping empty categories. Each bullet: date (if known) + plain-language summary of the event.

---

### 🎯 Recommended First Message Hook
One paragraph (3–5 sentences) of a ready-to-use cold outreach opener that references the top signal naturally, connects it to a business challenge, and ends with a low-friction call to action.

---

### ⏱ Timing Notes
1–3 bullets flagging any urgency factors (e.g., "Funding closed 3 weeks ago — budget window likely open," "New VP Sales started last month — still forming vendor opinions").

---

**Length:** Entire brief should fit on one page. Prioritize signal density over completeness. If no meaningful news exists in a category, omit it entirely.
