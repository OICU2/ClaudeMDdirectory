# Sales Methodology Adherence Audit

This skill reviews provided CRM data, call notes, and deal records against a specified sales methodology to surface qualification gaps, missing stakeholder coverage, and stage exit criteria violations.

## Audit Workflow

1. **Identify the methodology**: Confirm which framework applies — MEDDIC/MEDDICC, BANT, SPIN, Challenger, SPICED, or a custom process. If unspecified, ask before proceeding.

2. **Map methodology criteria to deal data**:
   - Extract each required element of the methodology (e.g., for MEDDIC: Metrics, Economic Buyer, Decision Criteria, Decision Process, Identify Pain, Champion).
   - For each element, locate evidence in the provided notes, call records, or CRM fields.
   - Mark each element as: ✅ Confirmed, ⚠️ Partial/Weak, or ❌ Missing.

3. **Check stage exit criteria**:
   - Identify what the current deal stage requires to be completed before progression.
   - Flag any deals that have advanced without meeting documented exit criteria.
   - Note if stage duration is anomalous (e.g., stuck longer than typical cycle).

4. **Assess stakeholder coverage**:
   - List all stakeholders mentioned in notes.
   - Identify which roles are present vs. absent (Economic Buyer, Champion, Technical Buyer, Legal, Procurement, End Users).
   - Flag deals where the Economic Buyer has not been engaged or confirmed.

5. **Identify qualification gaps**:
   - Highlight specific unanswered questions that the methodology requires.
   - Note where assumptions are being made instead of confirmed facts.
   - Flag deals progressing on activity alone (demos given, proposals sent) without validated need or fit.

6. **Cross-deal pattern analysis** (if multiple deals provided):
   - Identify which methodology elements are most frequently missing across the pipeline.
   - Note if gaps cluster by rep, segment, deal size, or stage.
   - Surface systemic coaching or process issues vs. one-off deal problems.

## Output Format

Produce a structured audit report with the following sections:

**Deal Audit Summary Table**
| Deal / Opportunity | Stage | Methodology Score | Critical Gaps | Risk Level |
|---|---|---|---|---|
One row per deal. Risk Level: 🔴 High / 🟡 Medium / 🟢 Low.

**Per-Deal Breakdown** (for each deal):
- Methodology element checklist (✅ / ⚠️ / ❌ with brief evidence or gap note)
- Stage exit criteria status
- Stakeholder map with missing roles called out
- Top 2–3 recommended next actions with specific questions to ask or information to obtain

**Pipeline-Level Findings** (if multiple deals):
- Most common missing methodology elements
- Deals at highest risk of stall or loss
- Coaching recommendations by pattern

Keep language direct and specific. Cite exact gaps using the rep's own words from notes where possible. Do not soften findings — name what is missing and why it creates deal risk.
