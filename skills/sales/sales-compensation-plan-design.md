# Sales Compensation Plan Design

This skill builds a complete, documented sales compensation plan including quota tiers, commission rates, accelerators, decelerators, and SPIFs aligned to company revenue targets and rep motivation goals.

## Plan Design Workflow

**1. Gather Inputs**
Collect these before designing:
- On-target earnings (OTE) and base/variable split (e.g., 60/40, 50/50)
- Annual quota per rep and total team quota
- Revenue targets and growth goals
- Deal types (new logo, expansion, renewal) and their relative priority
- Fiscal year structure (monthly, quarterly draws or payments)

**2. Define Quota Attainment Tiers**
Build at minimum 5 tiers:
- Below 50%: 0% commission (or recoverable draw)
- 50–74%: 50–60% of target rate (decelerator)
- 75–99%: 80–90% of target rate (approaching target)
- 100%: 100% target commission rate
- 101–120%: 125–150% (first accelerator)
- 121%+: 175–200% (kicker/stretch accelerator)

Specify whether tiers are cumulative (stack) or applied retroactively to all bookings.

**3. Set Commission Rates by Deal Type**
Assign differentiated rates to steer rep behavior:
- New logo: highest rate (e.g., 10–12% of ACV)
- Expansion/upsell: mid rate (e.g., 7–8%)
- Renewal: lowest rate (e.g., 3–5%)
- Multi-year deals: consider present-value discounting or cap at 2 years

**4. Design Accelerators**
- Define exact thresholds and multipliers (e.g., 120% quota = 1.5x rate on incremental bookings)
- Specify whether accelerators apply to all bookings or only incremental bookings above threshold
- Add a stretch kicker for blowout performance (e.g., 150%+ quota = 2x rate)
- Cap total variable payout if required by finance (document the cap explicitly)

**5. Build SPIF Programs**
- Define the objective: push a product, clear pipeline, accelerate a quarter
- Set duration: typically 30–90 days, never open-ended
- Structure reward: cash bonus, gift cards, experiences, or extra PTO
- Example: $500 bonus for every new logo closed in Q3 on Product X
- Document eligibility, payout timing, and stackability with base comp

**6. Add Clawback and Governance Rules**
- Clawback period: typically 90–180 days if deal cancels or customer churns within X days
- Define what triggers clawback (full churn, partial, non-payment)
- State payout timing: monthly, quarterly, or upon cash receipt
- Include a dispute resolution process and plan change notice period (90 days standard)

**7. Model the Plan**
Run three scenarios before finalizing:
- Conservative: 70% of team at 80% quota
- Target: 80% of team at 100% quota
- Upside: 20% of team at 130%+ quota
Confirm total comp cost stays within budget at each scenario.

## Output Format

Produce a structured compensation plan document with these sections:

**Section 1: Plan Summary**
- OTE, base, variable, quota per rep, plan year, payment frequency (2–3 sentences + table)

**Section 2: Attainment Tier Table**
Markdown table with columns: Attainment Range | Commission Rate | Multiplier | Example Payout at $X Quota

**Section 3: Deal Type Rate Card**
Markdown table
