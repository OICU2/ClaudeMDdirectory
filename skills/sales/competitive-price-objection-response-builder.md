# Competitive Price Objection Response Builder

This skill generates customized, ready-to-deliver response scripts that help sales reps reframe price objections by anchoring competitor pricing differences to concrete value gaps and ROI evidence.

## Response Building Workflow

1. **Extract the objection context** — Identify the specific competitor named, the stated price gap (dollar amount or percentage), the prospect's industry or role if provided, and the stage in the sales cycle.

2. **Clarify missing inputs if needed** — If no competitor is named, ask. If no value differentiators are provided, prompt the user to list 2–3 key advantages their product has (e.g., uptime, support, integrations, compliance). If ROI data exists, request it now.

3. **Map the price gap to value categories** — Categorize differentiators into: (a) cost avoidance, (b) revenue enablement, (c) risk reduction, or (d) time savings. Assign each differentiator to a category.

4. **Calculate or estimate ROI framing** — Convert the price difference into a per-user, per-day, or per-outcome figure. If ROI proof points are provided, calculate payback period or value multiple. Use conservative, credible numbers.

5. **Draft the response script in three tiers:**
   - **Acknowledge** — Validate the concern without conceding ground
   - **Reframe** — Shift focus from price to total cost of ownership or outcome value
   - **Prove** — Drop in the specific ROI figure, customer proof point, or risk comparison
   - **Close the micro-commitment** — End with a question that advances the conversation

6. **Generate objection variants** — Produce three script versions: (a) email/async, (b) live call, (c) one-liner for quick responses in chat or follow-up.

7. **Add handling tips** — Flag any common traps (e.g., discounting too early, over-explaining features) and suggest one follow-up question to uncover the real concern behind the price objection.

## Output Format

Produce a structured response document with the following sections:

**Objection Summary**
- Competitor named, stated price gap, deal context (1–2 lines)

**Value Mapping Table**
| Differentiator | Category | Quantified Impact |
|---|---|---|
| [feature/advantage] | [cost avoidance / revenue / risk / time] | [$X or X% or X hours saved] |

**Response Scripts**

*Live Call Script* (150–200 words, conversational tone, includes the acknowledge-reframe-prove-close structure)

*Email/Async Version* (100–150 words, slightly more formal, scannable with a clear CTA)

*One-Liner* (1–2 sentences, punchy, usable in Slack, LinkedIn, or verbal quick-response)

**Handling Tips**
- 2–3 bullet warnings about common mistakes in this specific scenario
- 1 discovery question to probe the real objection beneath price

Keep language direct, rep-ready, and free of jargon. Scripts should sound human, not like a pitch deck.
