# Deal Pause Re-Engagement Script Generator

This skill creates tailored, multi-channel re-engagement scripts for deals that have stalled or gone dark, giving sales reps a concrete outreach plan to restart momentum.

## Re-Engagement Script Workflow

### Step 1: Gather Deal Context
Before generating scripts, collect the following information (ask if not provided):
- **Contact name, title, and company**
- **Last interaction date** and what was discussed or agreed upon
- **Days/weeks since last response** (inactivity duration)
- **Deal stage** at the time of stall (e.g., demo done, proposal sent, negotiating)
- **Known pain points or priorities** from earlier conversations
- **Any external context** (company news, leadership changes, funding, layoffs, seasonality)
- **Preferred outreach channels** (email, LinkedIn, phone, SMS)

### Step 2: Diagnose the Stall Type
Classify the likely reason for silence to tailor tone and angle:
- **Priority shift** — buyer got pulled onto other initiatives
- **Internal blocker** — budget freeze, champion left, procurement delay
- **Evaluation fatigue** — too many vendors, decision paralysis
- **Ghosting after proposal** — price or scope concern not voiced
- **Unknown** — no clear signal, requires neutral re-entry

### Step 3: Apply Re-Engagement Rules
- **Do not apologize** for following up — frame outreach as adding value, not chasing
- **Reference something specific** from the last interaction to prove continuity
- **Introduce a new hook** — a relevant piece of news, a new case study, a changed circumstance, or a deadline
- **Make the ask small** — one question, one 15-minute call, one yes/no response
- **Vary channel and timing** across the sequence — do not repeat the same medium back-to-back
- **Set a graceful exit** at the final touchpoint — give them permission to say no cleanly

### Step 4: Build the Sequence
Generate a 3–5 touch sequence calibrated to inactivity duration:
- **14–30 days dark** → 3-touch sequence, warm and direct
- **31–60 days dark** → 4-touch sequence, reframe value, acknowledge gap
- **61+ days dark** → 5-touch sequence, treat as near-cold, re-establish context fully

---

## Output Format

Produce the following for each re-engagement sequence:

**Header Block**
- Contact name, company, stall stage, inactivity duration, diagnosed stall type

**Sequence Table**

| Touch # | Day | Channel | Subject / Opening Line | Core Message (2–3 sentences) | CTA |
|---------|-----|---------|----------------------|------------------------------|-----|

**Full Draft Scripts**
Write out each touchpoint in full, ready to send with minimal editing:
- Email: subject line + body (under 120 words)
- LinkedIn DM: under 75 words, casual register
- Phone voicemail: 20–30 second script, spoken cadence
- SMS (if applicable): under 50 words

**Personalization Callouts**
List 2–3 bracketed placeholders the rep should customize before sending (e.g., `[insert their recent product launch]`, `[reference the metric they mentioned]`).

**Coaching Note**
One sentence advising the rep on tone, timing, or the single most important thing to get right for this specific stall type.
