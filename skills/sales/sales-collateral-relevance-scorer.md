# Sales Collateral Relevance Scorer

Scores and ranks sales assets against a prospect's profile, industry, pain points, and current deal stage so reps always share the highest-impact content at the right moment.

## Scoring Workflow

### Step 1 — Capture Inputs
Collect the following before scoring. If any are missing, ask for them explicitly:
- **Prospect profile**: company name, industry, company size, key stakeholders/personas
- **Deal stage**: e.g., awareness, discovery, evaluation, proposal, negotiation, closed-lost re-engage
- **Known pain points or priorities**: verbatim notes from calls or emails preferred
- **Objections raised** (if any): price, integration complexity, timing, competitor preference
- **Assets available**: list each piece with a short description, content type (case study, one-pager, demo video, ROI calculator, whitepaper, etc.), and target persona/industry if known

### Step 2 — Score Each Asset (1–10) Across Four Dimensions
Apply these criteria to every asset provided:

| Dimension | What to Evaluate |
|---|---|
| **Stage Fit** | Does this asset serve the prospect's current buying stage? (e.g., thought-leadership for awareness, ROI calculators for evaluation) |
| **Industry/Persona Match** | Does it reference the prospect's vertical or speak to their role? |
| **Pain Point Alignment** | Does it directly address a stated concern, challenge, or priority? |
| **Objection Coverage** | Does it preemptively address or neutralize a known objection? |

Compute a **weighted total score**:
- Stage Fit: 30%
- Industry/Persona Match: 25%
- Pain Point Alignment: 30%
- Objection Coverage: 15%

### Step 3 — Rank and Select Top Recommendations
- Rank all assets by weighted score, highest to lowest
- Flag the **top 3 assets** as primary recommendations
- Flag 1–2 assets as **secondary / leave-behind** options
- Discard or explicitly deprioritize any asset scoring below 4.0 overall; note why

### Step 4 — Write Sharing Guidance for Each Top Asset
For each top-3 asset, provide:
- **Why it fits now**: one sentence tying the asset to current deal context
- **When to share it**: before the next call, during the meeting, as a follow-up, etc.
- **How to position it**: a 1–2 sentence talk track or email intro the rep can use verbatim
