# Sales Kickoff Presentation Builder

This skill drafts a complete, structured sales kickoff presentation covering wins, strategy, quota context, and team enablement themes to align and energize a sales team entering a new period.

## Workflow

1. **Gather inputs before drafting.** Ask the user for:
   - Sales period covered (Q1, H1, full year, etc.)
   - Top 2–3 wins or customer stories from the prior period
   - Strategic priorities or product focus areas for the upcoming period
   - Quota changes or territory updates (directional is fine)
   - Key enablement or training themes planned
   - Audience size and seniority (reps, managers, executives, mixed)
   - Tone preference (high-energy rally, analytical, balanced)

2. **Structure the presentation in logical sections.** Follow this sequence:
   - Opening hook and theme statement
   - State of the business (prior period recap, headline metrics)
   - Win highlights and customer stories (2–3 specific examples)
   - Strategic priorities for the upcoming period (3–5 pillars)
   - Quota and compensation context (framing, not raw numbers unless provided)
   - Competitive landscape snapshot (if information is provided)
   - Enablement and training roadmap
   - Team recognition segment
   - Call to action and closing rally

3. **Write each section as slide-ready content.** For each section produce:
   - A slide title
   - 3–5 bullet points or a short paragraph of speaker notes
   - A suggested visual or data callout where relevant

4. **Calibrate language to the tone and audience.** Use motivating, direct language for reps; add context and data framing for executives; avoid jargon unless the user's inputs use it naturally.

5. **Flag gaps explicitly.** If the user did not provide win stories, quota data, or competitive context, insert clearly marked placeholders (e.g., `[INSERT WIN STORY: customer name, deal size, key challenge solved]`) so nothing is accidentally omitted.

## Output Format

Produce a complete slide-by-slide outline using this structure for each slide:

```
## Slide [N]: [Slide Title]
**Headline:** [One bold statement or key message]
**Bullets / Speaker Notes:**
- [Point 1]
- [Point 2]
- [Point 3]
**Visual Suggestion:** [Chart type, image concept, or data callout]
```

- Total length: 10–14 slides
- Each slide section: 4–8 lines
- Include an executive summary block at the top listing the theme, period, and section count
- End with a "Next Steps" slide listing 3 immediate actions the sales leader should take after the kickoff
