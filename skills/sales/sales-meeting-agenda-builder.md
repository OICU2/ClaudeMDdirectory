# Sales Meeting Agenda Builder

This skill creates a structured, stage-appropriate meeting agenda — including objectives, tailored discovery questions, and desired next steps — whenever a user is preparing for a sales call or prospect conversation.

## Agenda Building Workflow

1. **Gather context before drafting.** If not already provided, ask for:
   - Prospect company name and industry
   - Pipeline stage (cold outreach, discovery, demo, proposal, negotiation, renewal)
   - Known pain points, goals, or prior conversation notes
   - Meeting duration and attendees (titles/roles)
   - Your product/service being sold

2. **Map the agenda to the pipeline stage.**
   - *Cold/First call:* Focus on rapport, qualification (BANT or MEDDIC), and earning the next meeting.
   - *Discovery:* Prioritize open-ended pain and impact questions; avoid pitching.
   - *Demo:* Tie feature walkthroughs to stated pain points; include confirmation checkpoints.
   - *Proposal/Negotiation:* Anticipate objections; confirm decision criteria and stakeholders.
   - *Renewal:* Review value delivered; surface expansion opportunities and risks.

3. **Write discovery questions that are specific to context.** Generic questions like "What are your challenges?" are not acceptable. Questions must reference the prospect's industry, role, or known situation.

4. **Define a concrete desired next step.** Every agenda must end with one specific action the rep wants to lock in before the call ends (e.g., "Schedule a technical deep-dive with the IT lead by Friday").

5. **Flag risks or gaps.** If critical context is missing (e.g., no known pain point, unknown decision-maker), note it as a preparation gap the rep should address before the call.

## Output Format

Produce a clean, scannable agenda with the following sections:

---

**Meeting Agenda: [Prospect Company] — [Meeting Type]**
**Date/Time:** [If provided, otherwise leave blank]
**Duration:** [X minutes]
**Attendees:** [Rep name] | [Prospect name, title]

---

**🎯 Meeting Objectives**
- [Primary goal — one sentence]
- [Secondary goal — one sentence]

**📋 Agenda Outline**
| Time | Topic |
|------|-------|
| 0–2 min | Introductions & agenda alignment |
| [X–X min] | [Section] |
| ... | ... |
| Last 5 min | Next steps & close |

**🔍 Discovery Questions**
1. [Specific, context-aware question]
2. [Specific, context-aware question]
3. [Specific, context-aware question]
4. [Specific, context-aware question]
5. [Specific, context-aware question]

**⚠️ Preparation Gaps**
- [Any missing info the rep should find before the call, or "None identified"]

**✅ Desired Next Step**
- [One specific, time-bound action to lock in before the call ends]

---

Keep the agenda to one page. Use plain language the rep can read at a glance during the call. Do not include generic filler questions or vague next steps.
