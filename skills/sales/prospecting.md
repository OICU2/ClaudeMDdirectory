# Sales Prospecting

This skill builds structured lists of qualified prospects by defining ideal customer profiles, identifying target segments, and generating actionable outreach-ready lead data.

## Prospecting Workflow

1. **Define the Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)**
   - Gather: industry, company size, geography, revenue range, tech stack, job titles
   - If not provided, ask 3 clarifying questions: Who is your best current customer? What problem do you solve? What's your deal size?

2. **Segment the Target Market**
   - Break the market into 2-4 distinct segments ranked by fit score (High / Medium / Low)
   - Assign fit rationale per segment based on ICP match

3. **Build the Prospect List**
   - Generate 10-25 example prospect entries per requested segment
   - Each entry includes: Company Name, Industry, Company Size, Location, Key Decision-Maker Title, Likely Pain Point, Recommended Outreach Channel
   - Flag accounts as Tier 1 (best fit), Tier 2 (good fit), or Tier 3 (exploratory)

4. **Identify Qualification Criteria**
   - List 5 BANT-aligned signals that indicate a prospect is sales-ready: Budget indicators, Authority signals, Need triggers, Timing cues
   - Include disqualification signals (red flags to skip)

5. **Suggest Data Sources**
   - Recommend 3-5 specific sources to find these prospects (e.g., LinkedIn Sales Navigator filters, G2 category pages, industry association directories, Crunchbase funding alerts, job posting signals)

6. **Generate Outreach Hooks**
   - Write 2-3 personalization angles per segment based on likely pain points and trigger events

## Output Format

Produce a structured prospect brief with these sections:

```
## Ideal Customer Profile Summary
[Bullet list of ICP criteria]

## Market Segments
| Segment | Fit Score | Rationale |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| ...     | High      | ...       |

## Sample Prospect List — [Segment Name]
| Company | Industry | Size | Location | Decision-Maker Title | Pain Point | Outreach Channel | Tier |
|---------|----------|------|----------|----------------------|------------|------------------|------|

## Qualification Signals
**Buy signals:** [5 bullets]
**Disqualifiers:** [3 bullets]

## Data Sources
[Numbered list with specific search tips per source]

## Outreach Hooks
[2-3 personalization angles with example opening lines]
```

Length: Full brief for 1-2 segments = 400-700 words. Scale up if multiple segments requested.
