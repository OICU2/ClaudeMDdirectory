# Contract Value Expansion Mapping

This skill systematically analyzes customer contract data to identify expansion signals, underutilized entitlements, and renewal windows so sales teams can prioritize and prepare for growth conversations.

## Expansion Analysis Workflow

**Step 1: Inventory Contracted Entitlements**
- List every product, feature, seat count, usage tier, and add-on included in the contract
- Flag items with hard caps vs. flexible limits
- Note any dormant or zero-usage line items

**Step 2: Map Utilization Against Entitlements**
- Compare current usage data to contracted limits for each entitlement
- Calculate utilization percentage per line item (e.g., 40 of 100 seats used = 40%)
- Classify each entitlement: Underutilized (<60%), Active (60–90%), At-Limit (>90%)

**Step 3: Identify Upsell Triggers**
- At-Limit items → immediate upgrade or overage conversation
- Underutilized items → adoption gap requiring success intervention before upsell
- Features not purchased but used adjacently → natural expansion pitch
- Contract clauses with volume discount thresholds not yet reached → cost-savings angle

**Step 4: Assess Renewal Window**
- Calculate days until contract end date
- Flag accounts within 180, 90, and 30-day renewal windows
- Identify auto-renewal clauses and opt-out deadlines
- Note any mid-term review or renegotiation rights

**Step 5: Prioritize Expansion Opportunities**
- Score each opportunity: (Utilization signal × Deal size potential × Renewal urgency)
- Rank accounts into tiers: Hot (act now), Warm (next 60 days), Nurture (plant seeds)
- Map each opportunity to the appropriate conversation type: Adoption, Upgrade, Cross-sell, or Renewal Renegotiation

**Step 6: Draft Expansion Hypotheses**
- For each top opportunity, write a one-sentence business case grounded in contract data
- Connect the expansion to a customer outcome, not a product feature

## Output Format

Produce a structured **Contract Expansion Map** with these sections:

---

**Account:** [Name] | **Contract End:** [Date] | **Days to Renewal:** [N]

**Entitlement Utilization Summary**
| Entitlement | Contracted | Current Usage | Utilization % | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| [Item] | [Limit] | [Usage] | [%] | Underutilized / Active / At-Limit |

**Top Expansion Opportunities** (ranked by priority)
1. [Opportunity type] — [One-line business case] — [Recommended action + timing]
2. ...
3. ...

**Renewal Risk Flags**
- [Any auto-renewal deadlines, opt-out windows, or contract landmines]

**Recommended Next Conversation**
- [Who to talk to, what to lead with, and which data point to anchor the conversation on]

---

Keep the map to one page per account. Use plain language. Every recommendation must cite a specific contract data point.
