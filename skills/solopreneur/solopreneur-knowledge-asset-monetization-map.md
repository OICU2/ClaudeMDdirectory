# Solopreneur Knowledge Asset Monetization Map

This skill audits a solopreneur's existing intellectual property — content, frameworks, templates, processes, and expertise — and produces a prioritized map matching each asset to its most viable monetization format.

## Asset Discovery & Mapping Workflow

### Step 1: Run the Asset Inventory Interview
Ask the user to list or describe what they have across these six categories. Prompt them explicitly if they skip any:

- **Content** — blog posts, newsletters, social threads, podcast episodes, videos
- **Frameworks** — repeatable thinking models, decision trees, named systems they use or teach
- **Processes/SOPs** — step-by-step workflows they follow to get results (for themselves or clients)
- **Templates** — documents, spreadsheets, Notion setups, prompt libraries, checklists
- **Lived expertise** — specific problems they have solved repeatedly, niches they have worked in
- **Audience signals** — questions they get asked most, DMs, testimonials, common client struggles

### Step 2: Score Each Asset on Three Axes
For each identified asset, evaluate:

1. **Specificity** (1–3): Is it generic advice or a specific, proven method with a clear outcome?
2. **Demand signal** (1–3): Has anyone asked for this, paid for adjacent help, or responded strongly to it?
3. **Production readiness** (1–3): How much work is required to turn it into a sellable product?

Total score out of 9 determines priority tier: 7–9 = Tier 1 (launch now), 4–6 = Tier 2 (develop next), 1–3 = Tier 3 (park or repurpose).

### Step 3: Match Each Asset to a Monetization Format
Use this matching logic:

| Asset Type | Best Monetization Format |
|---|---|
| Deep expertise + repeatable process | Paid workshop (live, $97–$497) or cohort course |
| Framework with clear steps | Self-paced mini-course or video breakdown ($27–$197) |
| SOP or workflow | Template or Notion/Airtable kit ($9–$79) |
| Collection of similar content | Paid newsletter tier, ebook, or resource vault |
| Lived transformation story + method | 1:1 coaching offer or group program |
| Frequently asked questions | FAQ guide, starter pack, or free lead magnet → paid upsell |
| Niche-specific process others can replicate | Done-for-you service productized into a fixed-price offer |

### Step 4: Identify the 90-Day Priority Stack
Select the top 3 assets from Tier 1 and sequence them:

- **Quick win (Week 1–2):** Lowest production effort, highest demand signal — likely a template or paid workshop
- **Core offer (Month 1–2):** Highest specificity asset packaged as a mini-course or productized service
- **Pipeline builder (Month 2–3):** Lead magnet or free content piece that feeds into the core offer

### Step 5: Flag Gaps and Leverage Points
Note any of the following if present:

- Assets with high specificity but no demand testing → recommend a presell or waitlist before building
- Duplicate assets that could be bundled into a higher-price offer
- Processes the user does for clients that aren't yet productized
- Frameworks with no name → recommend naming them to increase perceived value and memorability
