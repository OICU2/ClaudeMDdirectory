# Solopreneur Productization Readiness Scorer

Scores an existing service across five productization dimensions and delivers a prioritized action plan for turning it into a fixed, sellable offering.

## Scoring Workflow

**Step 1 — Gather Service Details**
If not already provided, ask for:
- What the service is and who it's for
- How a typical engagement starts, runs, and ends
- Current pricing model (hourly, project, retainer, or other)
- How much each delivery varies client to client
- Average number of deliverables per engagement

**Step 2 — Score Across Five Dimensions (1–10 each)**

Score each dimension using the criteria below. Be direct and numerical.

1. **Repeatability** — Does the work follow a consistent process, or does it start from scratch each time?
   - 1–3: Fully custom, no standard process
   - 4–6: Loosely repeatable with significant variation
   - 7–10: Clear, documented steps that work across clients

2. **Deliverability** — Can the outcome be produced in a predictable timeframe with predictable effort?
   - 1–3: Timeline and effort vary wildly
   - 4–6: Somewhat predictable but frequent exceptions
   - 7–10: Consistent scope, timeline, and effort every time

3. **Pricing Clarity** — Can a fixed price be set confidently without custom quoting?
   - 1–3: Every project requires a unique quote
   - 4–6: Ballpark exists but wide variance
   - 7–10: Clear, defensible fixed price is obvious

4. **Outcome Specificity** — Is the result of the service concrete, nameable, and tangible?
   - 1–3: Outcome is vague or highly subjective
   - 4–6: Outcome is somewhat defined but fuzzy at edges
   - 7–10: Client knows exactly what they'll receive

5. **Client Independence** — Can the service be delivered without heavy ongoing client involvement or custom input?
   - 1–3: Requires constant client collaboration to proceed
   - 4–6: Moderate back-and-forth required
   - 7–10: Minimal client input needed after onboarding

**Step 3 — Calculate Composite Score**
Add all five scores. Maximum is 50.
- **40–50**: Productization-ready. Move to packaging and pricing now.
- **28–39**: Mostly ready. Address the lowest-scoring dimensions before launching.
- **15–27**: Partially ready. Significant process or scope work needed first.
- **Below 15**: Not yet ready. Focus on standardizing the service before productizing.

**Step 4 — Identify Blockers and Quick Wins**
For each dimension scoring below 7:
- Name the specific blocker in one sentence
- Give one concrete action to raise that score within 30 days

**Step 5 — Recommend a Productized Format**
Based on the scores, recommend one of:
- **Fixed-scope project** (clear start/end, one price)
- **Productized retainer** (recurring fixed deliverables)
- **Tiered package** (2–3 clearly scoped tiers)
- **Template or DIY product** (if deliverability or client independence is very low)

Explain in 2–3 sentences why that format fits this service.

## Output Format

Produce a structured report with these sections in order:

---

**Productization Readiness Report**

**Service:** [Name or description of the service]

**Dimension Scores**
| Dimension | Score | Reasoning |
|---|---|---|
| Repeatability | X/10
