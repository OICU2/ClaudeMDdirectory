# Platform Dependency Risk Audit

This skill analyzes a solopreneur's current platform dependencies across leads, payments, and audience, scores their single-point-of-failure risk, and delivers a prioritized diversification action plan.

## Audit Workflow

### Step 1: Gather Dependency Data
Ask the user to answer these specific questions if not already provided:
- What platforms drive the majority of your leads or new customers? (e.g., Instagram, Google, Etsy, cold email)
- What percentage of your revenue runs through each payment processor or marketplace? (e.g., Stripe, PayPal, Amazon, Gumroad)
- Where does your audience primarily live? (e.g., email list, Facebook group, TikTok followers, Discord)
- Have you experienced any platform disruptions, bans, or algorithm changes in the past 2 years?
- Do you own your customer contact data (email/phone), or is it locked inside a platform?

### Step 2: Score Dependency Risk
Evaluate each of three categories on a 1–5 risk scale:

**Lead Generation Risk**
- 1 = Leads come from 4+ independent sources
- 3 = 50–70% of leads from one platform
- 5 = 90%+ of leads from a single platform or algorithm

**Payment & Revenue Risk**
- 1 = Revenue spread across 3+ processors/marketplaces with no single >40%
- 3 = One processor handles 60–80% of revenue
- 5 = Single processor or marketplace handles 90%+ of revenue or controls payouts

**Audience Ownership Risk**
- 1 = Owns a direct-contact list (email/SMS) of 1,000+ engaged subscribers
- 3 = Audience is split between owned and rented platforms
- 5 = Audience exists entirely on rented platforms with no owned contact data

### Step 3: Identify Critical Failure Scenarios
For each category scored 3 or higher, name the specific failure scenario:
- What happens if this platform bans the account tomorrow?
- What happens if this platform changes its algorithm or fee structure?
- What happens if this payment processor freezes funds for 30–90 days?

### Step 4: Generate Diversification Actions
For each high-risk dependency (score 3–5), produce 2–3 concrete actions ranked by effort and impact:

**Lead diversification options:** SEO/blog, email list building, referral program, secondary social platform, podcast, community, paid ads on a second channel.

**Payment diversification options:** Add a second processor (e.g., if Stripe, add PayPal or Lemon Squeezy), sell on an owned site alongside any marketplace, set up a cash reserve equivalent to 60 days of operating costs.

**Audience ownership options:** Launch or grow an email list with a lead magnet, migrate community to an owned platform (Circle, Substack, self-hosted), collect phone numbers via SMS opt-in, export and back up all customer data monthly.

### Step 5: Build a 90-Day Resilience Plan
Assign each action to one of three phases:
- **Days 1–30:** Emergency fixes — actions that reduce catastrophic risk immediately
- **Days 31–60:** Structural improvements — building owned channels and backups
- **Days 61–90:** Long-term diversification — establishing a second sustainable source
