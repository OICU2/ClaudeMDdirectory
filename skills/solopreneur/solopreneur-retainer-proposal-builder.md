# Solopreneur Retainer Proposal Builder

This skill generates a complete, client-ready monthly retainer proposal document by gathering key details about the client relationship, service scope, and pricing logic, then structuring it into a professional proposal ready to send or adapt.

## Proposal Building Workflow

### Step 1: Gather Client & Relationship Context
Ask the user for the following if not already provided:
- Client name, industry, and size
- Existing relationship (new prospect vs. current project client)
- The primary problem the client needs solved on an ongoing basis
- Any previous work done or conversations about ongoing support

### Step 2: Define the Retainer Scope
Help the user articulate:
- **Core deliverables**: What recurring outputs the client receives each month (e.g., 4 blog posts, 2 strategy calls, monthly analytics report)
- **Included activities**: Ongoing tasks, availability, or access (e.g., Slack access, priority response, monthly review)
- **Exclusions**: What is explicitly NOT covered to prevent scope creep
- **Capacity cap**: Hours or project units per month if applicable

### Step 3: Build Pricing Rationale
Calculate and justify pricing by:
- Estimating monthly hours or effort required
- Applying the user's hourly or day rate as a baseline
- Applying a retainer discount (typically 10–20%) to reward commitment
- Framing value in terms of client outcomes, not just time
- Noting what comparable project-based work would cost (to anchor value)

### Step 4: Define Terms & Conditions
Include:
- Contract length (e.g., 3-month minimum, rolling monthly)
- Notice period to cancel (e.g., 30 days written notice)
- Payment terms (e.g., invoiced on the 1st, due within 7 days)
- Rollover policy for unused hours/deliverables
- Revision or overage policy

### Step 5: Write the Proposal
Assemble all inputs into a full proposal document following the output format below.

## Output Format

Produce a complete retainer proposal with the following sections:

---

**MONTHLY RETAINER PROPOSAL**
*Prepared for: [Client Name] | Prepared by: [Solopreneur Name] | Date: [Date]*

---

**OVERVIEW**
2–3 sentences summarizing the purpose of this retainer and the ongoing problem it solves for the client.

**WHAT'S INCLUDED**
Bullet list of monthly deliverables and activities, written in plain, client-friendly language.

**WHAT'S NOT INCLUDED**
Short bullet list of explicit exclusions to prevent scope creep.

**INVESTMENT**
| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Monthly Retainer Fee | $X,XXX/month |
| Contract Term | X months minimum |
| Payment Due | [Date] each month |

Include 2–4 sentences of pricing rationale explaining the value and how it compares to project-based rates.

**TERMS**
- Notice to cancel: [X days] written notice
- Unused hours/deliverables: [rollover or forfeit policy]
- Overages: [hourly rate or pre-approval policy]
- Payment method: [invoice/Stripe/etc.]

**NEXT STEPS**
Clear 1–2 sentence call to action (e.g., "Reply to this proposal to confirm, and I'll send the contract and first invoice by [date].")

---

Proposal length: 400–700 words. Tone should be confident, specific, and professional — not salesy. Avoid vague language like "various tasks" or "as needed." Every deliverable and term must be concrete.
