# 90-Day Niche Authority Content Plan

This skill generates a structured 90-day content roadmap that maps topics to audience trust stages and distribution channels, helping solopreneurs systematically build niche authority without burning out.

## Workflow

### Step 1: Extract Niche Profile
Before planning, gather or infer:
- **Niche and sub-niche**: Be specific (not "fitness" — "strength training for women over 40")
- **Primary audience pain points**: What keeps them up at night?
- **Solopreneur's unique angle**: What lived experience or perspective do they own?
- **Current presence**: Where are they publishing now? What's working?
- **Available content hours per week**: Realistic output capacity

If any of these are missing from the prompt, ask before proceeding.

### Step 2: Map the Trust Ladder
Structure all content across three audience trust stages:

**Stage 1 — Awareness (Days 1–30): "Who are you?"**
- Goal: Make strangers realize you understand their problem better than they do
- Content tone: Educational, empathetic, no-pitch
- Topics: Address the most Googled/searched questions in the niche; debunk common myths; share a compelling origin story

**Stage 2 — Credibility (Days 31–60): "Can I trust you?"**
- Goal: Demonstrate depth, not just breadth
- Content tone: Opinionated, framework-driven, data-backed
- Topics: Contrarian takes, proprietary frameworks or mental models, case studies (own experience or anonymized clients), "how I actually do X" breakdowns

**Stage 3 — Authority (Days 61–90): "You're the one I follow"**
- Goal: Become the reference point in the niche
- Content tone: Visionary, community-building, invitation to dialogue
- Topics: Predictions, curated roundups with your editorial layer, collaborative content (interviews, quotes), calls-to-action toward email list or core offer

### Step 3: Assign Distribution Channels
For each piece of content, assign a primary and secondary channel based on niche behavior:

- **Long-form anchor content** (1x/week): Blog post, newsletter issue, YouTube video, or podcast episode — wherever the niche researches
- **Repurposed short-form** (3–5x/week): LinkedIn posts, Twitter/X threads, Instagram carousels, or TikToks — wherever the niche scrolls
- **Community seeding** (2x/week): Reddit, Slack groups, Discord servers, or niche forums — where the niche talks to each other
- **Email list** (1x/week): Must appear from Day 1; this is the owned channel that converts attention into authority

### Step 4: Build the 90-Day Content Calendar
Produce a week-by-week plan with:
- **Weekly theme** tied to trust stage
- **Anchor content topic** with working title and key angle
- **3 short-form derivatives** from that anchor
- **1 community engagement prompt** (question to post, thread to join, or discussion to start)
- **Email subject line** for that week's newsletter

### Step 5: Define Authority Signals to Track
Identify 3–5 metrics that indicate authority is building (not just vanity metrics):
- DMs from strangers asking for advice
- Being mentioned or quoted without prompting
- Inbound collaboration requests
- Email reply rate
- Search impressions for niche keywords
