# Solopreneur Skill Gap to Offer Mapper

Maps a solopreneur's existing capabilities against current market demand to instantly identify packagable offers and the highest-ROI skill gaps to close next.

## Skill Gap to Offer Mapping Workflow

### Step 1: Extract Current Capabilities
Ask the user to list (or infer from context):
- Hard skills (tools, platforms, technical abilities)
- Soft skills (communication, teaching, systems thinking)
- Domain knowledge (industries, niches, subjects)
- Past roles, projects, or results they've produced
- Credentials, certifications, or self-taught expertise

If the user hasn't provided this, prompt them: "Tell me what you've done professionally and what you're genuinely good at — including things you consider obvious or basic."

### Step 2: Pull In-Demand Market Services
Cross-reference the user's skills against high-demand solopreneur service categories:
- AI prompt engineering and workflow automation
- Short-form video scripting and editing
- SEO content strategy and writing
- Funnel and landing page copywriting
- LinkedIn ghostwriting and personal brand building
- No-code tool setup (Notion, Airtable, Zapier, Make)
- Financial modeling or bookkeeping for small businesses
- Email marketing setup and sequence writing
- UX audits and conversion optimization
- Online course creation and curriculum design
- Community building and moderation strategy
- B2B lead generation and cold outreach

### Step 3: Identify Immediate Offer Candidates
For each skill the user has that overlaps with market demand:
- Name a concrete service they could sell this week
- Suggest a delivery format (done-for-you, consulting, audit, template, course)
- Estimate a starter price range based on market rates
- Flag whether it's a fast-cash offer (quick to sell) or premium offer (higher ticket, longer sales cycle)

### Step 4: Identify High-Value Skill Gaps
For services with strong demand where the user is partially qualified:
- Name the specific missing skill or credential
- Rate the gap as: Small (1–4 weeks to close), Medium (1–3 months), or Large (3+ months)
- Recommend the fastest credible path to close it (course, practice project, certification, shadowing)
- Flag whether closing the gap unlocks a significantly higher price point

### Step 5: Prioritize and Recommend
Rank outputs into three buckets:
1. **Ship Now** — Offers the user can package and sell within 7 days using existing skills
2. **Close the Gap** — 1–2 skill gaps worth closing in the next 90 days with highest revenue upside
3. **Deprioritize** — Skills or gaps that are low-demand or require too much time to monetize soon

## Output Format

Produce a structured report with these sections:

**Your Immediate Offer Opportunities**
A numbered list of 2–4 services the user can offer now. Each entry includes:
- Service name (specific, not vague)
- Delivery format
- Suggested price range
- One-line pitch the user could use to sell it

**Your Top Skill Gaps to Close**
A table with columns: Skill Gap | Gap Size | Path to Close | Revenue Unlocked

**Your 90-Day Recommendation**
3–5 bullet points summarizing what to do first, what to build toward, and what to ignore for now.

Keep the total output under 600 words. Use plain language. No jargon unless the user introduced it first. If the user's background is unclear, ask one focused clarifying question before generating the report.
