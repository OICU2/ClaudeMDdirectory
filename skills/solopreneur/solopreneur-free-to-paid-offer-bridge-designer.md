# Free-to-Paid Offer Bridge Designer

This skill builds the complete messaging and conversion architecture that transforms free subscribers, community members, or lead magnet recipients into paying customers — covering the bridge narrative, email sequence, and transition touchpoints.

## Bridge Design Workflow

### Step 1: Diagnose the Gap
Ask for (or extract from context):
- What is the free asset? (lead magnet, community, newsletter, challenge)
- What is the paid offer? (course, coaching, membership, product)
- What transformation does each deliver?
- How long has the audience been in the free ecosystem?
- What conversion rate are they currently seeing (if any)?

### Step 2: Identify the Bridge Narrative
The bridge narrative is the single idea that makes the paid offer feel like the obvious next step — not a pitch.

Construct it using this formula:
- **The Result Gap**: What does the free asset help them achieve, and where does it leave them stuck?
- **The Belief Shift**: What does the audience need to believe is true before they'll pay?
- **The Natural Next Step**: Frame the paid offer as the fulfillment of what the free asset promised.

Example structure: "You now know [free asset outcome]. The reason most people still struggle with [problem] is [root cause the free asset doesn't solve]. That's exactly what [paid offer] is built to fix."

### Step 3: Design the Conversion Sequence
Build a 5-part email or content sequence:

1. **Welcome + Quick Win** (Day 0–1): Deliver the free asset. Give one immediate result. Establish credibility.
2. **The Problem Deepener** (Day 2–3): Surface the deeper problem the free asset reveals but doesn't solve. Use a story or case study.
3. **The Belief Bridge** (Day 4–5): Introduce the core idea behind the paid offer without pitching it. Shift their worldview.
4. **Social Proof + Transformation Story** (Day 6–7): Show someone who went from where the reader is now to where they want to be — using the paid offer.
5. **The Offer Introduction** (Day 8–10): Present the paid offer as the natural next step. Include clear CTA, what's included, and a reason to act now (deadline, bonus, or cohort).

### Step 4: Write the Transition Touchpoints
Identify every place in the free-to-paid journey where a message appears:
- Post-opt-in thank you page
- Email subject lines for each sequence email
- PS lines (these carry significant conversion weight — every email should have one nudging toward the offer)
- Community pinned posts or welcome messages (if applicable)

### Step 5: Anti-Friction Audit
Flag these common conversion killers and provide fixes:
- **Mismatch**: Free asset promises X, paid offer delivers Y — rewrite the bridge narrative
- **Too fast**: Pitching before delivering value — delay offer introduction by at least 3 touchpoints
- **Too vague**: Offer description doesn't connect to the pain the free asset surfaced — add specificity
- **No urgency**: Open-ended offer with no reason to act — add a time-bound element or enrollment window

## Output Format

Produce the following in order:

**1. Bridge Narrative (2–3 sentences)**
The core connecting idea between the free asset and paid offer. Ready to use as email copy or landing page text.

**2. Email Sequence Outline (5 emails)**
For each email:
- Subject line (2 options)
- Core message in 1 sentence
- Call-to-action
- PS line

**3. Thank You Page Script (100–150 words)**
What the subscriber sees immediately after opting in — plants the seed for the paid offer without
