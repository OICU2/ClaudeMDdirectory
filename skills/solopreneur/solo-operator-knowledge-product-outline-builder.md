# Knowledge Product Outline Builder

Extracts a solopreneur's core service expertise and structures it into a complete, market-ready outline for a course, workshop, or ebook — ready to build or sell immediately.

## Workflow

### Step 1: Extract the Expertise Core
Ask the solopreneur these questions (all at once, in a single message):
1. What is the primary service you sell or deliver to clients?
2. What is the single biggest transformation your clients experience after working with you?
3. What do you explain or teach clients repeatedly that they always find valuable?
4. Who is your ideal buyer for this product — beginner, intermediate, or advanced?
5. What format feels right to you: self-paced course, live workshop, or ebook?

### Step 2: Identify the Product Core
From their answers, extract:
- **The Promise**: One sentence stating the transformation the product delivers
- **The Audience**: Specific person this is for (job title, situation, pain point)
- **The Gap**: What they know vs. what they need to know
- **The Format**: Course (5–8 modules), Workshop (3–5 sections), or Ebook (5–8 chapters)

### Step 3: Build the Outline Structure
Use this framework regardless of format:

**Module/Chapter 1 — The Problem & The Promise**
- Why their current approach isn't working
- What becomes possible with this method
- Who this is for (and who it isn't)

**Modules/Chapters 2–[N-1] — The Core Method**
- Break the solopreneur's process into 3–6 discrete, teachable steps
- Each step = one module/chapter
- Name each step with an action verb + outcome (e.g., "Map Your Client's Hidden Trigger Points")
- Include 2–4 sub-topics per step drawn from what the solopreneur actually does

**Final Module/Chapter — Implementation & What's Next**
- How to apply the full method
- Common mistakes to avoid
- Next step or upgrade path (optional: leads to their service)

### Step 4: Add Sellable Metadata
For each module/chapter, generate:
- A benefit-driven title (not just a topic label)
- One sentence describing what the learner will be able to do after completing it
- One "quick win" the learner gets within the first 20% of the product

### Step 5: Suggest a Product Title
Generate 3 title options using this formula:
- **[Result] for [Audience] Without [Common Obstacle]**
- **The [Adjective] [Method/System/Blueprint] for [Transformation]**
- **How to [Achieve Outcome] Even If [Limiting Belief]**
