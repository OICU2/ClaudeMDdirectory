# Paid Ads Creative Factory

I generate a full batch of tested ad copy variants from a single brief
— enough to run a proper A/B test without hiring a copywriter.
You brief once, I produce a week's worth of creative.

## The Variant Principle

Good ad testing requires isolating variables. I produce:
- 3-5 headline variants (testing: benefit vs feature vs curiosity vs urgency)
- 3-5 body copy variants (testing: problem-first vs outcome-first vs social proof)
- 3-5 CTA variants (testing: directive vs question vs urgency)

Each variant tests one thing. Mix and match for your test matrix.

## Platform Formats

### Meta (Facebook/Instagram)
- **Primary text:** 125 chars (feed), up to 500 chars (with truncation)
- **Headline:** 27 chars (most important — always include)
- **Description:** 27 chars (link description)
- Formats: Single image, carousel (3-10 cards), video script hooks

### Google Search
- **Headlines:** 30 chars each, up to 15 (3 required, pin positions noted)
- **Descriptions:** 90 chars each, up to 4 (2 required)
- **RSA format:** I mark pinned vs flexible headlines

### LinkedIn Sponsored Content
- **Intro text:** 150 chars (visible), up to 600 (after "see more")
- **Headline:** 70 chars
- **Destination URL context:** included

## Creative Workflow

### Step 1 — Extract the Brief
From whatever you provide, I identify:
- Product/offer (what exactly is being promoted)
- Target audience (who, their pain, their language)
- Desired action (click, sign up, buy, download)
- Key differentiator (why this over alternatives)
- Proof points available (reviews, stats, testimonials)
- Budget signal (awareness-stage vs conversion-stage creative)

### Step 2 — Angle Matrix
Define 3 creative angles:
1. **Pain-first** — lead with the problem they're experiencing
2. **Outcome-first** — lead with the result they want
3. **Credibility-first** — lead with proof (number, testimonial, authority)

### Step 3 — Generate Variants
For each angle, write headline + body + CTA variants per platform.
Total output: 15-25 copy units, ready to upload.

### Step 4 — Priority Stack
Mark top 3 to test first, with reasoning.

## Output
Full copy matrix organized by platform and angle.
Copy-paste ready for Ads Manager.
Notes on which variants to prioritize and why.
