# Solo Operator Yearly Business Review Builder

This skill guides solopreneurs through building a complete, structured annual business review by asking targeted questions and compiling the answers into a clear, shareable document.

## Review Building Workflow

**Step 1 — Gather Core Financials**
Ask the user to provide:
- Total revenue this year vs. last year (or first-year baseline)
- Primary revenue streams and their approximate percentage of total income
- Biggest single revenue month and why it spiked
- Months where revenue dipped and the likely cause

**Step 2 — Identify Top Clients or Customers**
Ask for:
- Top 3–5 clients or customer segments by revenue or impact
- Which relationships grew, stayed flat, or ended
- Any clients they want to replicate or avoid next year

**Step 3 — Evaluate Offer Performance**
Ask for:
- Every active offer, product, or service sold this year
- Which offers sold most easily and which required heavy lifting
- Any offers launched and abandoned — and why
- What the market responded to most enthusiastically

**Step 4 — Extract Lessons Learned**
Prompt the user with these specific questions:
- What was the biggest mistake and what did it cost (time, money, energy)?
- What would you do differently if you started January over?
- What surprised you — positively or negatively?
- What drained you most, and what energized you?

**Step 5 — Define Goals for the Coming Year**
Ask for:
- Primary revenue target and the logic behind it
- One offer or income stream to build, kill, or double down on
- One operational or personal change to make
- A word or theme for the coming year

**Step 6 — Compile the Review**
Assemble all answers into the structured document described below. Do not editorialize — reflect the user's own words back clearly and concisely.

## Output Format

Produce a titled Markdown document with these exact sections:

```
# [Year] Annual Business Review — [Name or "Your Business"]

## Financial Snapshot
- Total Revenue: $X
- vs. Prior Year: +X% / -X% / first year
- Top Revenue Stream: [name] (~X% of total)
- Peak Month: [month] — [1-sentence reason]
- Low Month: [month] — [1-sentence reason]

## Top Clients & Relationships
- [Client/Segment 1]: [1-sentence description of relationship and value]
- [Client/Segment 2]: ...
- [Client/Segment 3]: ...
- Clients to replicate: [brief note]
- Clients to avoid: [brief note]

## Offer Performance
| Offer | Result | Keep / Cut / Grow |
|-------|--------|-------------------|
| [Offer 1] | [sold well / struggled / abandoned] | [decision] |
| [Offer 2] | ... | ... |

## Lessons Learned
1. [Biggest mistake and cost]
2. [What you'd do differently]
3. [Biggest surprise]
4. [What drained vs. energized you]

## Goals for [Next Year]
- Revenue Target: $X — [1-sentence rationale]
- Offer Focus: [build / kill / double down on X]
- Operational Change: [specific change]
- Year Theme: "[word or phrase]"

## One-Paragraph Summary
[3–5 sentences synthesizing the year: what was built, what was learned, and where the business is headed. Written in second person to feel personal and direct.]
```

Length: Concise but complete. Each section should be scannable in under 60 seconds. The full document should fit on 1–2 printed pages.
