# Client Result Documentation System

This skill runs a structured capture workflow immediately after project completion to extract, store, and format client outcomes into ready-to-use testimonials and case studies before the details fade.

## Result Capture Workflow

**Step 1: Trigger the Capture (Do this within 48 hours of project completion)**

Ask the operator these questions and record their answers verbatim:

1. What was the client's situation or problem before the project started? (Be specific: numbers, pain points, timeline)
2. What did you deliver or do for them?
3. What changed for the client after the project? (Look for: revenue, time saved, costs reduced, stress removed, goal achieved)
4. Did the client say anything notable — in email, Slack, or conversation — about the results?
5. Would the client give a public testimonial, a LinkedIn recommendation, or a private reference?

**Step 2: Extract the Core Result Statement**

From the answers, identify:
- The **before metric or condition** (e.g., "spending 10 hours/week on invoicing")
- The **after metric or condition** (e.g., "down to 45 minutes")
- The **emotional or business impact** (e.g., "freed up time to take on two new clients")

If numbers are missing, prompt: "Can you estimate a percentage improvement, hours saved, or dollar amount — even a rough one?"

**Step 3: Generate Three Asset Formats**

Produce all three outputs simultaneously (see Output Format below).

**Step 4: Store in a Running Log**

Append a one-line summary entry formatted for a master results log:

```
[Date] | [Client Industry] | [Service Delivered] | [Key Result] | [Asset Status: Draft/Approved/Published]
```

Remind the operator to paste this into their Client Results Log (a simple doc, Notion page, or spreadsheet).

**Step 5: Flag Follow-Up Actions**

State clearly which of these applies and what to do next:
- If client has not been asked for a testimonial: provide a copy-paste request message
- If client approved sharing: mark assets as ready to publish
- If numbers are estimates: note they need client confirmation before publishing

## Output Format

Produce all three assets in clearly labeled sections:

---

**ASSET 1: SHORT TESTIMONIAL (for website, proposal, or LinkedIn)**
2–4 sentences. Written in first person as if from the client. Captures the before state, the result, and an emotional close. Clearly marked as a draft for client approval.

Example structure:
> "Before working with [Name], I was [problem]. After [deliverable], [specific result]. Now [new state]. I'd recommend them to anyone who [target client situation]."

---

**ASSET 2: CASE STUDY SUMMARY (for portfolio or sales conversations)**
150–250 words. Structure:
- **Client:** Industry, size, or role (anonymized if needed)
- **Challenge:** What problem they had before
- **Solution:** What the operator delivered and how
- **Result:** Specific outcomes with numbers or clear qualitative wins
- **Timeline:** How long the project took

---

**ASSET 3: ONE-LINE SOCIAL PROOF SNIPPET (for cold outreach, proposals, or bios)**
One sentence, punchy, result-first. No fluff.

Example: "Helped a 3-person e-commerce brand cut fulfillment errors by 60% in 6 weeks."

---

**FOLLOW-UP ACTION REQUIRED:**
State exactly what the operator needs to do next (send approval request, confirm a number, or publish).
