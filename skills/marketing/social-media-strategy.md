# Social Media Strategy

This skill builds a platform-specific social media plan covering channel selection, content pillars, posting cadence, and growth tactics based on the user's goals, audience, and resources.

## Strategy Development Workflow

1. **Clarify the foundation** — Identify: (a) brand/person/product being promoted, (b) primary goal (awareness, leads, sales, community, personal brand), (c) target audience demographics and interests, (d) available resources (time per week, budget, team size), (e) current presence and baseline metrics if any.

2. **Select platforms** — Recommend 2–3 platforms maximum based on:
   - Where the target audience spends time
   - Content format strengths (video → TikTok/Reels/YouTube Shorts; B2B → LinkedIn; visual products → Instagram/Pinterest; real-time/news → X/Twitter; community → Discord/Reddit)
   - Realistic production capacity
   - Explain why each selected platform is included and why others are deprioritized.

3. **Define content pillars** — Create 3–5 recurring content themes that align with brand identity and audience needs. Each pillar should serve a distinct purpose (educate, entertain, inspire, convert, build trust).

4. **Build the content calendar framework** — Specify:
   - Posting frequency per platform (realistic, not aspirational)
   - Content format mix per platform (e.g., 50% short video, 30% carousels, 20% text posts)
   - Optimal posting times based on platform data and audience timezone
   - Ratio of original vs. curated vs. promotional content (e.g., 70/20/10 rule)

5. **Define growth tactics** — Provide 3–5 specific, platform-appropriate tactics such as:
   - Hashtag strategy (number, mix of niche/broad)
   - Engagement loops (comment reply windows, community engagement hours)
   - Collaboration or cross-promotion opportunities
   - Paid amplification thresholds (when/if to boost)
   - Algorithm-specific behaviors to leverage (e.g., early engagement signals, watch-time optimization)

6. **Set KPIs and review cadence** — Define 2–3 measurable goals per platform (follower growth rate, engagement rate, reach, click-through, saves) and recommend a 30/60/90-day review schedule.

## Output Format

Produce a structured strategy document with these sections:

**1. Strategy Overview** (3–4 sentences summarizing goals, audience, and approach)

**2. Platform Selection** (table or bullet list: Platform | Why Selected | Primary Content Type | Goal)

**3. Content Pillars** (numbered list of 3–5 pillars, each with: name, description, 2 example post ideas)

**4. Content Calendar Framework** (per platform: posting frequency, format breakdown, best times, content ratio)

**5. Growth Tactics** (5–7 specific, actionable tactics with brief explanation of each)

**6. KPIs & Milestones** (table: Metric | Baseline | 30-day target | 90-day target | per platform)

**7. Quick Wins** (3 things the user can do in the next 7 days to start momentum)

Length: Medium-to-long form (600–1000 words). Use headers, bullets, and tables for scannability. Avoid vague advice — every recommendation must be specific and implementable.
