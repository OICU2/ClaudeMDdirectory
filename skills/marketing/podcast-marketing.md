# Podcast Marketing Strategy

This skill builds end-to-end podcast marketing strategies that turn audio content into measurable business outcomes — audience growth, leads, and brand authority.

## Podcast Marketing Workflow

### 1. Define the Podcast's Business Purpose
- Identify the primary goal: brand awareness, lead generation, customer retention, or sales enablement
- Map the podcast audience to the buyer persona (job title, industry, pain points, stage in funnel)
- Choose a positioning angle: interview show, solo commentary, panel, or narrative storytelling
- Set 90-day success metrics: downloads per episode, email subscribers, qualified leads, partnership inquiries

### 2. Name, Format, and Positioning
- Generate 3–5 show name options: keyword-rich, memorable, searchable on Spotify/Apple
- Recommend episode length based on audience (15–20 min for busy professionals, 45–60 min for enthusiasts)
- Define episode cadence: weekly is standard; biweekly is acceptable for solo shows
- Write a 1-sentence show premise: "[Podcast name] helps [target audience] achieve [outcome] through [format]"

### 3. Content Strategy and Guest Pipeline
- Identify 8–12 cornerstone episode topics that map to buyer pain points
- Create a guest sourcing framework: target criteria, outreach message template, booking workflow
- Plan solo vs. interview episode ratio (suggested: 70% interviews, 30% solo for credibility balance)
- Build a content repurposing chain: episode → blog post → LinkedIn clips → email newsletter → quote graphics

### 4. Distribution and Growth Tactics
- List required distribution channels: Apple Podcasts, Spotify, Amazon Music, Google Podcasts
- Specify SEO tactics: keyword-optimized episode titles, show notes with transcript excerpts, chapter markers
- Outline cross-promotion strategies: guest swaps, newsletter mentions, community shares
- Define a launch strategy: release 3 episodes on day one, run a review campaign for first 30 days

### 5. Lead Generation Integration
- Place a single, specific CTA in every episode (lead magnet, free audit, waitlist, newsletter)
- Create a podcast landing page with episode archive, host bio, and CTA above the fold
- Set up listener email capture via episode-specific lead magnets or bonus content
- Track attribution: UTM links, unique promo codes, or dedicated landing page per episode

### 6. Monetization Path (if applicable)
- Outline monetization tiers: sponsorships (1,000+ downloads/episode), premium content, community, services
- Draft a sponsor pitch one-pager: audience demographics, download stats, ad placement options, rates
- Recommend dynamic ad insertion vs. host-read spots based on show maturity

## Output Format

Produce a structured Podcast Marketing Plan with the following sections:

1. **Show Brief** — Name, premise sentence, target audience, primary business goal, success metrics
2. **Format Spec** — Episode length, cadence, format type, host/guest structure
3. **First 10 Episodes** — Episode titles with guest type or topic angle for each
4. **Distribution Checklist** — Platforms, SEO requirements, launch sequence
5. **Growth Playbook** — 3 specific growth tactics with execution steps
6. **Lead Gen System** — CTA, landing page spec, email capture method, attribution approach
7. **Repurposing Pipeline** — Step-by-step chain from raw audio to 4+ content assets

Length: comprehensive enough to act on immediately — typically 600–1,000 words. Use headers, bullet points, and numbered lists. No paragraphs of generic advice. Every item must be specific to the user's industry, audience, and goal.
