# Product Review Response Templates

This skill generates customizable, brand-consistent response templates for positive, negative, and neutral product reviews across platforms like Amazon, Google, Yelp, App Store, and Trustpilot.

## Workflow

1. **Gather brand context** — Ask for brand name, tone (formal/casual/friendly), industry, and any specific phrases or values to include or avoid. If not provided, use neutral professional defaults.

2. **Identify review categories** — Generate templates for all three types unless the user specifies otherwise:
   - **Positive** (4–5 stars, enthusiastic feedback)
   - **Neutral** (3 stars, mixed or lukewarm feedback)
   - **Negative** (1–2 stars, complaints or criticism)

3. **Apply platform awareness** — Note character limits and norms per platform:
   - Google/Yelp: conversational, community-facing
   - Amazon: policy-compliant, no off-platform contact requests
   - App Store: brief, empathetic, often technical
   - Trustpilot: professional, detail-oriented

4. **Build each template with these components:**
   - **Opening**: Personalized acknowledgment using reviewer's name placeholder `[Name]`
   - **Validation**: Reflect the customer's specific experience (positive or negative)
   - **Action or appreciation**: Thank, explain next steps, or offer resolution path
   - **Brand reinforcement**: One sentence connecting to brand values or commitment
   - **Closing**: Friendly sign-off with `[Team/Rep Name]` placeholder

5. **Negative review rules:**
   - Never be defensive or dismissive
   - Acknowledge the issue without admitting legal liability
   - Move resolution offline: include `[support email/phone]` placeholder
   - Offer a concrete next step, not vague promises

6. **Create variants** — Provide 2 variants per category (concise and detailed) so teams can match response length to review depth.

7. **Add customization notes** — After each template, include bracketed instructions explaining which fields to swap and what to personalize.

## Output Format

Produce a structured document with the following sections:

```
## Positive Review Templates

**Variant A (Concise)**
[Template text with [Name], [Product], [Team Name] placeholders]
*Customization notes: ...*

**Variant B (Detailed)**
[Template text]
*Customization notes: ...*

---

## Neutral Review Templates

**Variant A (Concise)**
...

**Variant B (Detailed)**
...

---

## Negative Review Templates

**Variant A (Concise)**
...

**Variant B (Detailed)**
...

---

## Platform-Specific Notes
- Google Reviews: [guidance]
- Amazon: [guidance]
- App Store: [guidance]
- Yelp: [guidance]
- Trustpilot: [guidance]
```

- Templates should be 50–150 words each (concise) or 100–250 words (detailed)
- All placeholders use `[Bracket Format]`
- Tone must be consistent across all templates for the same brand
- End with a one-paragraph summary of best practices for the brand's review response strategy
