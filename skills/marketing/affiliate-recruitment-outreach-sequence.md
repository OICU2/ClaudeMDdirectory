# Affiliate Recruitment Outreach Sequence

This skill generates a complete multi-step cold outreach sequence for recruiting affiliate partners, including personalized pitch angles, program benefit summaries, and follow-up cadence — activated when someone needs to build or scale their affiliate partner base.

## Workflow

### Step 1: Gather Program Details
Before building the sequence, collect or infer:
- **Product/service** being promoted
- **Commission structure** (percentage, flat fee, recurring)
- **Target affiliate profile** (bloggers, influencers, newsletters, review sites, etc.)
- **Unique program benefits** (high AOV, long cookie window, dedicated affiliate manager, custom landing pages)
- **Any exclusivity or bonuses** for early or top performers

### Step 2: Define the Affiliate Persona
Identify the ideal affiliate partner:
- Their content niche and audience overlap with the product
- Their monetization goals (passive income, high commissions, brand alignment)
- Their likely objections (low conversions, complex sign-up, untrustworthy brands)
- Where they are being found (Instagram, YouTube, niche blogs, LinkedIn)

### Step 3: Build the Outreach Sequence

Produce a **5-touch sequence** with the following structure:

**Email 1 — The Warm Opener (Day 1)**
- Subject line referencing their specific content or audience
- One genuine compliment tied to a specific piece of their work
- One-sentence pitch connecting their audience to the product
- Soft CTA: "Would it make sense to send you details?"

**Email 2 — The Value Drop (Day 3)**
- Lead with a concrete program benefit (e.g., "Our affiliates average $X/month")
- Include a social proof stat or testimonial from an existing affiliate if available
- Restate the audience fit angle
- CTA: link to affiliate program page or offer to jump on a quick call

**Email 3 — The Differentiator (Day 7)**
- Highlight one thing that makes the program better than alternatives (cookie duration, commission tier, support)
- Address the most common objection for this affiliate type
- CTA: "I can set up your account today if you're interested"

**Email 4 — The Case Study / Proof Point (Day 14)**
- Share a brief story or stat: "One of our affiliates in [similar niche] earned $X in their first 30 days"
- Keep it under 100 words
- CTA: direct sign-up link with a deadline-based bonus if applicable

**Email 5 — The Graceful Breakup (Day 21)**
- Acknowledge this may not be a fit right now
- Leave the door open with a resource or evergreen link
- No hard sell — preserve the relationship for future outreach

### Step 4: Personalization Variables
Flag placeholders in each email using `[BRACKETS]`:
- `[AFFILIATE_NAME]`
- `[THEIR_CONTENT_EXAMPLE]`
- `[NICHE]`
- `[COMMISSION_RATE]`
- `[PROGRAM_LINK]`
- `[BONUS_OFFER]`

### Step 5: Subject Line Variants
Generate 3 subject line options per email optimized for:
- Curiosity
- Specificity
- Benefit-led framing

## Output Format

Produce a structured document with the following sections:

1. **Affiliate Persona Summary** — 3–5 bullet points describing the target affiliate type
2. **Sequence Overview Table** — Day, Email #, Goal, and CTA for each touch
3. **Full Email Copy** — Each of the 5 emails written in full, with subject line variants (3 per email), body copy (100–200 words each), and labeled placeholders
4. **Program Benefit Snapshot** — A 4–6 bullet
