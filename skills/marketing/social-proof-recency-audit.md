# Social Proof Recency Audit

This skill systematically reviews all forms of published social proof to flag outdated, irrelevant, or potentially damaging content that could undermine brand credibility and buyer trust.

## Audit Workflow

### 1. Inventory Collection
Request or accept a list of all social proof assets, including:
- Testimonials (website, landing pages, email, ads)
- Case studies (PDFs, web pages, sales decks)
- Third-party reviews (G2, Trustpilot, Capterra, Google, Yelp, etc.)
- Press quotes and media mentions
- Star ratings and aggregate review scores
- Social media screenshots used as proof

### 2. Flag by Recency Threshold
Apply these age-based risk ratings to each asset:

| Age | Risk Level | Action |
|-----|-----------|--------|
| 0–12 months | ✅ Current | Keep as-is |
| 13–24 months | ⚠️ Monitor | Review for relevance |
| 25–36 months | 🔶 Stale | Update or retire |
| 36+ months | 🔴 Outdated | Remove or replace immediately |

### 3. Contextual Relevance Check
Beyond age, flag any asset where:
- The customer company has been acquired, rebranded, or shut down
- The quoted contact has left the company or changed roles
- The product/feature referenced no longer exists or has changed significantly
- The results cited (e.g., "saved 40 hours/week") are not achievable with the current product
- Industry context has shifted (e.g., pre-pandemic stats used post-pandemic)
- Pricing or market claims are no longer accurate

### 4. Credibility Risk Assessment
For each flagged item, assess secondary risk factors:
- Is this testimonial prominently placed (hero section, homepage, ads)?
- Is the customer name/logo easily verifiable online?
- Does the claim make a specific, falsifiable assertion?
- Could a skeptical buyer Google this and find contradicting information?

### 5. Prioritize Removal Queue
Rank flagged items by: (placement prominence × risk level × verifiability risk)
- **Immediate remove:** Outdated + prominent + falsifiable
- **Update within 30 days:** Stale + prominent OR outdated + buried
- **Archive for review:** Monitor-tier items older than 18 months

### 6. Replacement Recommendations
For each removed item, suggest:
- The type of replacement needed (industry, use case, company size match)
- Outreach angle to get a fresh quote from the same customer if relationship exists
- Whether a case study refresh is warranted vs. a new customer story

## Output Format

Produce a structured audit report with the following sections:

**Executive Summary**
- Total assets reviewed
- Count by risk level (Current / Monitor / Stale / Outdated)
- Top 3 highest-priority actions

**Asset-by-Asset Audit Table**
For each item:
- Asset name/location
- Type (testimonial / case study / review / press quote)
- Date published or last verified
- Age in months
- Risk level (✅ / ⚠️ / 🔶 / 🔴)
- Specific flag reason (1 sentence)
- Recommended action (Keep / Monitor / Update / Remove)

**Priority Removal Queue**
Ordered list of the top items to remove or update immediately, with rationale.

**Replacement Roadmap**
Table of gaps created by removals, with suggested customer profiles to target for new social proof collection.

**Quick Wins**
2–5 low-effort actions (e.g., remove a specific outdated logo, update a date-stamped quote) that can be done in under an hour.
