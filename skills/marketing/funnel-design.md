# Funnel Design

This skill maps and builds complete marketing funnels—defining each stage, the content/tactics for that stage, conversion goals, and handoff criteria—so prospects move predictably from awareness to purchase.

## Funnel Design Workflow

1. **Identify the product and audience** — Clarify what is being sold, who the ideal buyer is, their pain points, and their awareness level (cold, warm, or hot).

2. **Define funnel stages** — Map the journey using these standard stages, customized to the business:
   - **Awareness** — Prospect discovers the brand (ads, SEO, social, referrals)
   - **Interest** — Prospect engages with content or lead magnet
   - **Consideration** — Prospect evaluates the offer (case studies, demos, comparisons)
   - **Intent** — Prospect signals buying readiness (free trial, consultation, cart)
   - **Purchase** — Prospect converts to customer
   - **Retention/Advocacy** — Customer becomes repeat buyer or referrer

3. **Assign tactics to each stage** — For every stage, specify:
   - Traffic source or activation method
   - Content type (ad, blog, email, webinar, landing page, etc.)
   - Primary call-to-action (CTA)
   - Conversion metric to track

4. **Define stage entry and exit criteria** — State what behavior or action moves a prospect to the next stage and what triggers removal or re-targeting.

5. **Identify friction points** — Flag where prospects are most likely to drop off and recommend a fix (e.g., too many form fields, weak social proof, unclear CTA).

6. **Set conversion benchmarks** — Provide realistic industry-standard conversion rates for each stage transition so the client knows what to aim for.

7. **Recommend tools** — Suggest specific platforms for each stage (e.g., Meta Ads for awareness, ConvertKit for nurture, Calendly for consultation booking, Stripe for purchase).

## Output Format

Produce a structured funnel document with these sections:

**Funnel Overview**
- Product/offer name
- Target audience (1–2 sentences)
- Funnel type (e.g., webinar funnel, product launch funnel, lead gen funnel)

**Stage-by-Stage Breakdown** (table or numbered list)
For each stage: Stage name | Goal | Tactics | CTA | Metric | Benchmark conversion rate

**Friction Point Analysis**
Bullet list of 3–5 likely drop-off points and specific fixes for each.

**Recommended Tech Stack**
Tool name → purpose → stage it serves (bullet list).

**Quick-Start Priority List**
3 actions to implement first, ranked by impact-to-effort ratio.

Length: 400–700 words. Use headers, tables, and bullets. No prose paragraphs except the funnel overview.
