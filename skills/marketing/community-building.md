# Community Building

This skill creates actionable community growth strategies and engagement plans when someone wants to build, launch, or scale an online community around their brand or product.

## Community Building Workflow

### 1. Define Community Foundation
- Identify the community's core purpose (support, learning, networking, advocacy)
- Define the target member persona: who they are, what they need, what they'll gain
- Choose the right platform based on audience behavior (Discord, Slack, Circle, Reddit, Facebook Groups, LinkedIn)
- Establish community values and rules that reinforce brand identity

### 2. Design the Onboarding Experience
- Create a welcome sequence: introduction post, resource pinned message, first action prompt
- Assign a welcome role or badge to reduce drop-off in the first 48 hours
- Set up an introduction channel or thread so new members feel seen immediately
- Write a community manifesto or "start here" guide that communicates belonging

### 3. Build a Content and Engagement Calendar
- Schedule 3 repeating content formats per week (e.g., Monday wins, Wednesday Q&A, Friday resource drops)
- Plan monthly events: live sessions, AMAs, challenges, or member spotlights
- Rotate content ownership: member-generated content reduces moderator burden and increases investment
- Use engagement loops: ask questions, run polls, celebrate milestones

### 4. Recruit and Retain Members
- Identify founding member strategy: recruit 20–50 highly engaged early members before public launch
- Create an exclusive benefit for early members (founding badge, lifetime discount, direct access)
- Set a 30/60/90 day retention target and track weekly active members as the north star metric
- Re-engage dormant members with a direct message, exclusive content, or a comeback challenge

### 5. Moderate and Scale
- Define clear moderation guidelines and consequences for violations
- Recruit 2–3 community moderators or ambassadors from your most engaged members
- Review community health monthly: post frequency, new member growth, churn rate, top contributors
- Introduce tiered membership or subgroups as the community scales past 500 members

## Output Format

Produce a **Community Building Plan** structured as follows:

1. **Community Identity** (3–5 bullet points): purpose, target member, platform recommendation, core values
2. **Onboarding Flow** (numbered steps): exact welcome sequence with sample copy for the first message
3. **90-Day Engagement Calendar** (table or list by week): content themes, event types, engagement tactics
4. **Growth Strategy** (bullet list): founding member approach, referral mechanics, cross-promotion channels
5. **Health Metrics Dashboard** (bullet list): 5 KPIs to track with suggested targets
6. **Quick Wins** (3 actions to take in the first 7 days)

Keep the plan specific to the brand, product, or niche provided. Use concrete examples, not generic advice. Total length: 400–700 words.
