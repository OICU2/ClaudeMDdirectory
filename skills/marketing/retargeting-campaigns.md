# Retargeting Campaign Builder

This skill creates complete retargeting campaign strategies with audience segments, ad sequence logic, copy, and budget guidance to re-engage visitors who didn't convert.

## Campaign Build Workflow

### 1. Identify Audience Segments
Define distinct retargeting pools based on user behavior:
- **Site visitors (no action):** Visited 1–2 pages, bounced within 30 seconds
- **Engaged visitors:** Spent >60 seconds, viewed 3+ pages, or visited pricing/product pages
- **Cart abandoners:** Added to cart but did not reach checkout
- **Checkout abandoners:** Reached checkout but did not complete purchase
- **Past customers:** Purchased 90–365 days ago (win-back segment)
- **Email non-openers:** On list but haven't engaged in 60+ days

### 2. Define Sequence Logic Per Segment

**Cart/Checkout Abandoners (highest intent):**
- Day 1–2: Direct product reminder, show exact item abandoned, include urgency (limited stock, sale ending)
- Day 3–5: Social proof ad (reviews, ratings, testimonials for that product)
- Day 6–10: Objection-handling ad (free returns, guarantee, FAQ-style copy)
- Day 11–14: Discount or incentive offer (10% off, free shipping)

**Engaged Visitors (medium intent):**
- Day 1–3: Brand awareness + value proposition ad
- Day 4–7: Best-seller or category-specific product showcase
- Day 8–14: Lead magnet or soft offer (free guide, quiz, email signup)
- Day 15–21: Testimonial or case study ad

**Past Customers (win-back):**
- Week 1: "We miss you" message + new arrivals or updated products
- Week 2: Loyalty reward or exclusive returning-customer offer
- Week 3: Cross-sell based on previous purchase category

**Cold Site Visitors (low intent):**
- Run 2-touch sequence only: brand story ad → social proof ad
- Exclude after 21 days if no engagement

### 3. Write Ad Copy for Each Touch

For each ad in the sequence, produce:
- **Headline:** 6–10 words, direct and benefit-led
- **Primary text:** 2–3 sentences max, address where the user is in the funnel
- **CTA:** Specific action (Shop Now, Claim Offer, See Reviews, Get 10% Off)
- **Creative direction:** Static image, carousel, or video recommendation with rationale

Follow these copy rules:
- Touch 1 never leads with a discount — lead with value or reminder
- Only introduce discounts at touch 3 or later
- Cart abandonment ads must name or show the specific product
- Win-back ads use emotional/relational tone, not aggressive sales language

### 4. Set Frequency Caps and Exclusions
- Cap impressions at 3–5 per user per day per segment
- Exclude recent purchasers (last 14 days) from all non-win-back campaigns
- Exclude current email subscribers from cold visitor campaigns
- Set campaign end dates: 7 days for abandoners, 21 days for engaged visitors, 30 days for win-back

### 5. Budget Allocation Guidance
Distribute retargeting budget by segment priority:
- Cart/Checkout abandoners: 40%
- Engaged visitors: 30%
- Past customers: 20%
- Cold visitors: 10%

Recommend platform based on product type:
- E-commerce physical goods → Meta (Facebook/Instagram) + Google Display
- B2B or high-ticket → LinkedIn Retargeting + Google Display
- App or software → Meta + YouTube pre-roll
