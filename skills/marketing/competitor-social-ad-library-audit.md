# Competitor Social Ad Library Audit

This skill conducts a structured audit of competitor ad libraries across Meta, TikTok, LinkedIn, and Google to surface actionable patterns in messaging, creative formats, hooks, and offer structures.

## Audit Workflow

### 1. Define Scope
- Confirm which competitors to audit (up to 5 per session)
- Confirm which platforms to cover: Meta Ad Library, TikTok Creative Center, LinkedIn Ad Library, Google Ads Transparency Center
- Confirm the analysis goal: messaging gaps, offer benchmarking, creative inspiration, or full competitive map

### 2. Platform-by-Platform Data Collection

**Meta Ad Library** (ads.facebook.com/ads/library)
- Search brand name, filter by active ads
- Note: ad volume (proxy for spend), launch dates (recency signals spend confidence), formats used (image/video/carousel/collection)
- Flag ads running 30+ days — these are likely winners

**TikTok Creative Center** (ads.tiktok.com/business/creativecenter)
- Search brand or industry keywords
- Note: hook styles (question/shock/demo/testimonial), video length, text overlay patterns, music choices

**LinkedIn Ad Library** (linkedin.com/ad-library)
- Search company name
- Note: audience signals from creative copy (job titles mentioned, pain points), CTA types, offer framing (demo/trial/report/webinar)

**Google Ads Transparency Center** (adstransparency.google.com)
- Search advertiser name
- Note: search vs. display vs. YouTube split, headline formulas, landing page themes from display creatives

### 3. Extract Patterns Across These Dimensions

**Messaging Patterns**
- Primary value proposition (what they lead with)
- Pain points explicitly named
- Social proof types used (numbers, logos, testimonials, awards)
- Urgency or scarcity language

**Offer Structures**
- Entry-point offer (free trial, demo, lead magnet, discount)
- Price anchoring language
- Guarantee or risk-reversal language

**Creative Formats**
- Dominant format per platform
- Hook formulas (first 3 seconds for video; headline for static)
- CTA button text patterns

**Funnel Signals**
- Are they running awareness + retargeting, or only one layer?
- Do landing page themes differ by ad type?

### 4. Identify Gaps and Opportunities
- What pain points are competitors NOT addressing?
- What formats are underused in the category?
- What offers are oversaturated (everyone running free trial = low differentiation)?
- Which competitor appears to be testing most aggressively (highest ad volume variance)?
