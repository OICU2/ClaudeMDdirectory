# Audience Building

This skill creates actionable audience growth strategies across owned channels — email, social, and community — so creators and businesses reduce dependency on rented platforms.

## Audience Building Workflow

### 1. Audit Current Position
Ask for (or infer) the following before strategizing:
- Existing channels and follower/subscriber counts
- What they create or sell (content type, niche)
- Where their target audience currently spends time
- Time and resource constraints (solo creator vs. team)

### 2. Define the Owned Audience Hierarchy
Prioritize channels in this order — owned > rented:
- **Tier 1 (Own):** Email list, SMS list, private community (Circle, Discord, Slack)
- **Tier 2 (Amplify):** SEO-driven blog, podcast, YouTube (algorithm-resistant)
- **Tier 3 (Rent):** Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok (use to drive Tier 1)

Always push toward converting rented followers into owned subscribers.

### 3. Build the Lead Magnet Stack
Identify or create at least one lead magnet per awareness stage:
- **Cold audience:** Standalone value (checklist, template, free tool, mini-course)
- **Warm audience:** Deeper content (case study, webinar, email course)
- **Hot audience:** Trial, consultation, sample of paid product

Lead magnet rules:
- Solves one specific problem in under 10 minutes
- Delivers immediate value before any purchase
- Directly relates to the paid offer downstream

### 4. Design the Growth Loop
Map a repeatable cycle:
1. **Create** — Publish content on Tier 3 platforms (social posts, short videos, threads)
2. **Capture** — CTA in every piece drives to lead magnet or email opt-in
3. **Nurture** — Welcome sequence (5–7 emails) delivers value and builds trust
4. **Activate** — Encourage subscribers to share, refer, or join community
5. **Repeat** — Repurpose top-performing content across channels

### 5. Email List Growth Tactics (Priority)
- Add opt-in to every content asset, bio link, and footer
- Run a 30-day "list sprint": post daily on one platform, all CTAs go to one opt-in
- Cross-promote with 2–3 adjacent newsletters (swap mentions or do a collab issue)
- Use content upgrades (post-specific bonuses that require an email to unlock)
- Pin opt-in link across all social profiles

### 6. Community Channel Strategy
- Choose one community platform only — avoid spreading thin
- Define the "reason to gather": shared goal, identity, or problem
- Seed with 20–50 founding members before opening publicly
- Create a weekly ritual (thread, challenge, live call) to drive recurring engagement
- Gate community access behind email subscription to grow both simultaneously

### 7. Social Channel Strategy (Rented — Use to Feed Owned)
- Pick one primary platform based on where the audience already lives
- Post at least 3x/week with explicit CTAs to owned channels
- Use platform-native formats (Reels, Shorts, carousels) for maximum reach
- Never post the full value on social — always leave a reason to subscribe

### 8. Set 30/60/90 Day Milestones
- **Day 30:** Lead magnet live, opt-in page converting at 20%+, welcome sequence written
- **Day 60:** 100+ new subscribers, first collab or cross-promotion done, community seeded
- **Day 90:** Consistent weekly email sending, growth loop documented, first referral mechanism tested
