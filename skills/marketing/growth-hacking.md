# Growth Hacking Skill

Generates unconventional, data-driven growth tactics tailored to the product, audience, and growth stage — prioritizing speed, scalability, and low cost over traditional marketing.

## Growth Hacking Workflow

1. **Diagnose the growth stage** — Determine if the product needs awareness (top of funnel), activation (first value moment), retention, referral, or revenue optimization. Ask if not clear.

2. **Identify the core user persona** — Who is the ideal early adopter? Where do they already spend time online? What pain point does the product solve in under 10 seconds?

3. **Map existing assets** — What does the team have right now: existing users, an email list, an API, a community, a dataset, a brand story, a unique integration point?

4. **Generate 5–10 specific growth tactics** using these proven growth hacking categories:
   - **Embedded virality** — Make sharing native to product usage (e.g., Hotmail footer, Calendly invite link)
   - **Platform piggybacking** — Ride existing networks (e.g., Airbnb/Craigslist, PayPal/eBay)
   - **Content SEO loops** — Create programmatic or user-generated content that compounds (e.g., Yelp, Glassdoor)
   - **Community infiltration** — Identify subreddits, Slack groups, Discord servers, or forums where the target user lives and provide genuine value
   - **Reverse trials / freemium wedges** — Give away a tool, template, or feature that hooks users into a paid funnel
   - **Partnership/co-marketing** — Find non-competing products with the same audience and cross-promote
   - **Referral mechanics** — Design incentive structures with two-sided rewards (referrer + referee)
   - **Data/PR stunts** — Publish a study, leak an insight, or build a free tool that earns press

5. **Score each tactic** on three dimensions:
   - **Effort** (Low / Medium / High)
   - **Time to first result** (Days / Weeks / Months)
   - **Scalability** (One-time / Repeatable / Compounding)

6. **Select the top 3 tactics** most suited to the product's current constraints and recommend an execution order.

7. **Define a 30-day action plan** for the top tactic with weekly milestones and one measurable north-star metric.

## Output Format

Produce a structured response with these sections:

**Growth Stage Assessment**
One sentence identifying the current bottleneck (acquisition / activation / retention / referral / revenue).

**Top 10 Tactics**
Numbered list. Each entry includes:
- Tactic name (bold)
- One-sentence description of the mechanic
- Effort | Time to Result | Scalability scores

**Recommended Top 3**
Short paragraph explaining why these three fit the product's stage and constraints.

**30-Day Execution Plan for Tactic #1**
- Week 1: [specific action]
- Week 2: [specific action]
- Week 3: [specific action]
- Week 4: [specific action]
- North-star metric to track: [metric]

Keep total length under 600 words. Use plain language. No generic advice like "post on social media" without specifics.
