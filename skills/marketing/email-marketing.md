# Email Marketing Campaign Design

This skill builds complete email marketing campaigns — from strategy and segmentation to subject lines and sequences — whenever someone needs to engage subscribers and drive revenue through email.

## Campaign Development Workflow

1. **Define campaign goal** — Clarify the objective: welcome series, promotional blast, abandoned cart, re-engagement, or product launch. Each goal drives a different structure.

2. **Identify audience segment** — Determine who receives this campaign (new subscribers, cold leads, active customers, lapsed buyers). Tailor tone and offer to that segment's position in the funnel.

3. **Map the sequence** — For drip/automation campaigns, define:
   - Number of emails (typically 3–7 for nurture sequences)
   - Send timing and spacing (e.g., Day 0, Day 2, Day 5, Day 10)
   - Trigger event (signup, purchase, inactivity threshold)

4. **Write subject lines** — Produce 3 subject line variants per email using proven frameworks:
   - Curiosity gap ("You're missing this one thing…")
   - Specificity + benefit ("3 ways to cut churn by 40% this quarter")
   - Direct urgency ("Last chance: offer ends tonight")

5. **Write email body copy** — Structure each email:
   - Opening hook (1–2 sentences tied to subject line)
   - Value-focused body (problem → insight → solution)
   - Single, clear CTA with action-oriented button text
   - P.S. line for secondary reinforcement

6. **Define success metrics** — Specify benchmarks to track: open rate target (industry avg 20–35%), CTR (2–5%), conversion rate, unsubscribe rate threshold.

7. **Apply deliverability best practices** — Flag any risks: spam trigger words, missing plain-text version, CAN-SPAM/GDPR compliance requirements (unsubscribe link, physical address).

## Output Format

Produce a complete campaign package with the following structure:

**Campaign Brief**
- Goal, target segment, sequence length, send schedule

**Email 1 of N: [Purpose Label]**
- Subject line (3 variants, labeled A/B/C)
- Preview text (40–90 characters)
- Body copy (150–300 words, formatted with clear paragraphs)
- CTA button text
- P.S. line

Repeat the email block for each message in the sequence.

**Performance Benchmarks**
- Expected open rate, CTR, and conversion rate ranges

**Deliverability Checklist**
- Bulleted list of compliance and technical items to verify before sending
