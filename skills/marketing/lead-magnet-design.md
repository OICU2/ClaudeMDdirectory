# Lead Magnet Design

This skill creates a complete lead magnet strategy — including format, topic, title, content outline, and landing page copy — to attract and convert the right target audience into subscribers or leads.

## Lead Magnet Design Workflow

### Step 1: Define the Ideal Lead Profile
- Identify the specific audience segment to attract (job title, pain point, buying stage)
- Clarify what problem they are actively trying to solve right now
- Determine what they are willing to exchange contact info to receive
- Establish what qualifies them as a good lead for this business

### Step 2: Select the Right Format
Match format to audience and business context:
- **Checklist/Cheat Sheet** — Quick wins, tactical audiences, low time investment
- **Template/Swipe File** — Saves time, practical professionals, high perceived value
- **Mini Guide/Report** — Thought leadership, B2B buyers, research-oriented audiences
- **Email Course (5–7 days)** — Nurtures leads over time, complex topics, builds trust
- **Webinar/Video Training** — High-ticket offers, visual learners, demo-driven products
- **Quiz/Assessment** — Personalized results, self-aware audiences, engagement-driven
- **Free Tool/Calculator** — ROI-focused buyers, technical audiences, ongoing utility
- **Resource Library** — Broad topic coverage, content-heavy brands

Choose format based on: speed to consume, immediate applicability, and alignment with the core product or service.

### Step 3: Validate the Topic
The topic must satisfy all four criteria:
1. **Specific** — Solves one narrow problem, not a broad category
2. **Immediately useful** — Delivers value within minutes or hours of download
3. **Related to the paid offer** — Creates a natural next step toward purchase
4. **Unique angle** — Differs from what competitors freely publish

Reject generic topics (e.g., "10 Marketing Tips"). Require specificity (e.g., "The 5-Email Sequence That Recovers Abandoned Carts for Shopify Stores Under $1M Revenue").

### Step 4: Write the Title
Use one of these proven title structures:
- **How to [Achieve Outcome] Without [Common Obstacle]**
- **The [Number]-Step [Process] for [Specific Result]**
- **[Number] [Format] Every [Audience] Needs to [Goal]**
- **The [Adjective] Guide to [Topic] for [Audience]**
- **[Action Verb] Your [Thing] in [Timeframe]: A [Format]**

Generate 3 title options. Select the strongest based on clarity and desire.

### Step 5: Build the Content Outline
Structure the lead magnet content:
- **Hook** — State the problem and promise the outcome (2–3 sentences)
- **Credibility bridge** — Why this source/method is trustworthy (1 sentence)
- **Core sections** — 3–7 actionable sections, each with a clear takeaway
- **Quick win** — Place one immediately actionable tip in the first section
- **Soft CTA** — Natural transition to the paid product/service at the end

### Step 6: Write the Opt-In Page Copy
Produce these elements:
- **Headline** — Lead with the outcome, not the format
- **Subheadline** — Name the specific audience and the specific pain point
- **Bullet list (3–5 bullets)** — "You'll discover..." statements tied to specific sections
- **Call-to-action button text** — Action verb + outcome (not "Submit" or "Download")
- **Trust element** — One-line privacy reassurance or social proof stat

### Step 7: Define the Follow-Up Trigger
Specify the first email or action after opt-in
