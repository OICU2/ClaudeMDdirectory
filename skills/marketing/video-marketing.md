# Video Marketing Strategy & Content Planning

This skill builds complete video marketing plans — from concept and scripting to distribution and performance tracking — whenever someone needs to grow an audience or convert viewers through video content.

## Video Marketing Workflow

### 1. Define Goals and Audience
- Identify primary objective: brand awareness, lead generation, product sales, or community building
- Define target viewer persona: demographics, pain points, platforms they use
- Set measurable KPIs: views, watch time, click-through rate, subscriber growth, conversion rate

### 2. Choose Platform and Format
- **YouTube**: long-form tutorials (8–20 min), series, evergreen content — optimized for search
- **Instagram Reels / TikTok**: short-form (15–90 sec), trending hooks, entertainment-first
- **LinkedIn**: thought leadership, case studies, B2B demos (1–5 min)
- **Email / Landing Page**: product demos, testimonials, explainer videos
- Match format to funnel stage: awareness (entertaining/educational), consideration (demos/comparisons), decision (testimonials/offers)

### 3. Build a Content Calendar
- Plan 4-week rolling calendar with 3–5 video topics per week
- Assign each video: platform, format, goal, primary keyword or hook
- Batch production: script and film 4–8 videos per session to reduce overhead
- Schedule publish times based on platform peak hours (e.g., YouTube: Thu–Sat, 2–4pm local)

### 4. Script and Structure Each Video
- **Hook (0–5 sec)**: state the outcome or provoke curiosity — never start with "Hey guys"
- **Setup (5–30 sec)**: establish credibility and preview the value
- **Body**: deliver the core content in numbered steps or clear segments
- **CTA (final 15–20 sec)**: one specific action — subscribe, click link, download, buy
- Keep scripts tight: aim for 130–150 words per minute of video

### 5. Optimize for Discovery
- Write keyword-rich titles: lead with the search term, add intrigue ("How to X — Without Y")
- Thumbnails: high contrast, 1–3 words max, face with emotion if applicable
- Description: first 2 sentences must contain keyword and CTA; include timestamps and links
- Tags: 5–10 specific tags per video, mix broad and niche terms

### 6. Distribution and Repurposing
- Repurpose each video into: short clip (Reels/Shorts), quote graphic, blog post, email snippet
- Share to all owned channels within 48 hours of publish
- Engage with every comment in the first 3 hours to boost algorithmic reach
- Run paid promotion on top-performing organic videos (boost posts with >5% engagement rate)

### 7. Analyze and Iterate
- Review analytics weekly: watch time retention, CTR, top traffic sources
- Drop or revise any content with <30% average view duration
- Double down on formats and topics with highest watch time and conversion
- A/B test thumbnails and titles on YouTube using two posted versions or third-party tools

## Output Format

For each video marketing request, produce:

1. **Strategy Summary** (3–5 bullet points): goals, audience, platforms, KPIs
2. **4-Week Content Calendar** (table format): Week | Video Title | Platform | Format | Goal | Publish Day
3. **Sample Script** for 1 priority video:
   - Hook (exact opening line)
   - Outline with timestamps
   - CTA (exact closing line)
4. **Optimization Checklist** per video: title formula, thumbnail guidance, description template, tags list
5. **Distribution Plan**: where, when, and how to repurpose each video
6. **Success Metrics Table**: KPI | Baseline | 30-Day Target | 90-Day Target

Keep language direct and production-ready. Avoid vague advice — every recommendation must be immediately actionable.
