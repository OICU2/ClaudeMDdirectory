# Influencer Content Brief Generator

Produces a complete, ready-to-send creative brief for influencer partners whenever a user needs to prepare a creator for a brand collaboration, sponsored post, or ambassador campaign.

## Brief Generation Workflow

1. **Gather campaign essentials before writing.** If any of these are missing, ask upfront: brand/product name, campaign goal (awareness, conversion, trial), target audience, platforms (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, etc.), content formats required (Reel, Story, long-form video, static post), and campaign dates.

2. **Define the product story.** Identify 3–5 core talking points the influencer must communicate. Each point should be one concrete, conversational sentence — not marketing jargon. Flag which points are mandatory vs. optional.

3. **Set tone and voice parameters.** Specify the brand voice (e.g., playful but not sarcastic, authoritative but approachable). List 3–5 words that match the tone and 3–5 words that are off-brand. Note if the creator should use their own voice freely within guardrails.

4. **Document all legal and compliance requirements.** Include the exact FTC/ASA disclosure language required (e.g., "#ad", "#sponsored", "Paid partnership with [Brand]"). Specify where in the content the disclosure must appear (first frame of video, first line of caption). Note any claims the influencer must NOT make (health claims, price guarantees, competitor comparisons).

5. **Specify content do's and don'ts.** List visual requirements (brand colors, product placement, logo visibility), prohibited content (competitors, controversial topics, specific imagery), and any brand asset links (logos, fonts, approved product shots).

6. **Write the call-to-action requirements.** Provide the exact CTA copy or acceptable variations, the link or promo code to use, and where it must appear (bio link, swipe-up, pinned comment, video description).

7. **Define deliverables and approval process.** Specify number of posts, content formats, draft submission deadline, revision rounds allowed, and go-live date. Note whether brand approval is required before posting.

## Output Format

Produce a brief in the following structure using clear headers. Length should be 400–700 words. Use bullet points for lists, bold for required vs. optional items.

---

**INFLUENCER CONTENT BRIEF**
**Campaign:** [Name] | **Brand:** [Brand] | **Dates:** [Draft due → Go-live]

[One sentence stating the primary objective and success metric]
