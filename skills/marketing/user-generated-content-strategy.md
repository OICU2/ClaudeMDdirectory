# UGC Strategy Builder

This skill designs complete user-generated content systems — from initial incentive structures through collection, curation, and multi-channel repurposing — activated when a brand needs authentic customer content at scale.

## UGC Strategy Framework

### 1. Audit Current UGC Landscape
- Identify where customers already talk about the brand (reviews, social tags, forums, support tickets)
- Assess what content types exist (photos, videos, reviews, stories, tutorials)
- Benchmark volume and quality against category competitors
- Flag legal and rights-management gaps in current process

### 2. Define UGC Goals and Content Gaps
- Map which funnel stages lack social proof (awareness, consideration, conversion, retention)
- Identify highest-value content types for the brand's category (unboxing, before/after, how-to, lifestyle)
- Set specific targets: volume per month, content types needed, quality thresholds
- Align UGC goals to measurable business outcomes (CAC reduction, conversion rate lift, email CTR)

### 3. Build the Incentive Architecture
- **Intrinsic incentives**: community recognition, brand features, creator badges, early access
- **Extrinsic incentives**: discounts, cash rewards, product gifting, affiliate commissions
- **Structural incentives**: post-purchase email flows, packaging inserts, hashtag campaigns, review request timing
- Match incentive type to audience motivation (community-driven vs. deal-seeking)
- Define minimum viable incentive to avoid cheapening brand perception

### 4. Design Collection Systems
- Create a branded hashtag with clear submission guidelines
- Build post-purchase automation sequence: request at day 7, reminder at day 14, final ask at day 30
- Set up review collection on owned channels (site, email, SMS) and third-party platforms
- Create a dedicated UGC submission portal or landing page for high-intent creators
- Define intake form fields to capture usage rights at point of submission

### 5. Establish Curation and Rights Management
- Define quality criteria: resolution minimums, brand safety rules, tone alignment
- Build a moderation workflow with approval tiers (auto-approve, manual review, reject)
- Draft standardized rights request language for social DM outreach
- Implement a UGC asset library with tagging by product, use case, channel, and creator type
- Set content expiration and refresh cadence

### 6. Create the Repurposing Playbook
- Map each UGC type to specific channel applications:
  - Reviews → product pages, email, paid ads
  - Photos/videos → social feed, Stories, paid social, website galleries
  - Testimonials → case studies, sales decks, landing pages
  - Community posts → newsletter features, PR pitches
- Define adaptation rules per channel (crop ratios, caption formats, tagging)
- Build a content calendar slot allocation for UGC vs. brand-produced content ratio

### 7. Measure and Iterate
- Track UGC submission rate, approval rate, and time-to-publish
- Measure performance of UGC vs. brand content on CTR, conversion, and engagement
- A/B test incentive types, request timing, and content formats quarterly
- Survey creators on experience to improve program participation

**1. Situation Summary** (3–5 bullets on current state and gaps)
