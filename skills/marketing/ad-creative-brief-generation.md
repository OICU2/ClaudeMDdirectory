# Ad Creative Brief Generation

This skill produces complete, structured creative briefs for ad campaigns so designers and copywriters can execute immediately without back-and-forth clarification.

## Brief Generation Workflow

1. **Extract core campaign inputs** from what the user provides: product/service, target audience, campaign goal, budget tier (if mentioned), channels, and timeline. If the goal is ambiguous, infer the most likely objective (awareness, conversion, retention) and state your assumption.

2. **Define the audience segment** with specificity: demographics, psychographics, pain points, and where they are in the funnel. Avoid vague descriptors like "millennials" — anchor to behaviors and motivations.

3. **Identify the single primary hook** — the one insight, tension, or desire the ad should exploit. Every asset must be buildable from this hook. Do not list multiple hooks; force a choice.

4. **Specify format requirements** for each channel mentioned: dimensions, length (video in seconds, copy in word count), static vs. motion, and primary placement (feed, story, banner, pre-roll, etc.).

5. **Write the messaging hierarchy**: headline direction (tone + angle), primary copy guidance (what to say, what to avoid), and CTA options (give 2–3 concrete CTA phrases, not categories).

6. **Set creative direction guardrails**: visual tone (provide 3 adjectives), color/imagery do's and don'ts, and any brand constraints the user mentioned. If none were mentioned, note that brand guidelines should be attached.

7. **Define success metrics** tied to the campaign goal: primary KPI, secondary KPI, and a benchmark or target if the user provided budget/performance context.

8. **Flag open questions** — list any missing inputs that would materially change the brief (e.g., no landing page URL, no budget, no brand voice reference). Keep this list short and specific.

## Output Format

Produce the brief as a structured document with these labeled sections in order:

```

Date: [today's date]
Prepared for: [team or stakeholder if mentioned, otherwise "Creative Team"]

─────────────────────────────────────────
CAMPAIGN OVERVIEW
Goal: [1 sentence]
Timeline: [dates or "TBD"]
Channels: [list]
Budget Tier: [if known, else "Not specified"]

─────────────────────────────────────────
TARGET AUDIENCE
Primary segment: [2–3 sentences — who they are, what they want, what's in their way]
Funnel stage: [Awareness / Consideration / Conversion / Retention]

[1–2 sentences. The core insight this campaign exploits.]
