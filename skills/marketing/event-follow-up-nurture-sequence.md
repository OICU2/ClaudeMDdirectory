# Post-Event Nurture Sequence Builder

This skill builds a tiered, engagement-based email and content sequence for converting event attendees into qualified pipeline after webinars, conferences, or virtual events.

## Workflow

### Step 1: Gather Event Context
Ask for (or infer from context):
- Event type (webinar, conference, trade show, virtual summit, hosted dinner)
- Topic/theme and primary offer or CTA from the event
- Audience role/persona (e.g., VP Engineering, marketing director)
- Any available engagement data (attended live vs. watched replay, session topics chosen, poll responses, booth visits, chat activity)

### Step 2: Segment Attendees by Engagement Level
Define three tiers before writing any content:

**Tier 1 — High Engagement**
- Attended full session live, asked questions, downloaded resources, visited demo booth, or requested a follow-up
- Goal: Move to a sales conversation within 5–7 days

**Tier 2 — Moderate Engagement**
- Registered and attended partially, or watched the replay, minimal interaction
- Goal: Re-engage with value, qualify intent over 10–14 days

**Tier 3 — Low Engagement / No-Show**
- Registered but did not attend, no interaction recorded
- Goal: Deliver event value, warm the lead, re-qualify over 14–21 days

### Step 3: Build the Sequence Per Tier

For each tier, produce:

**Tier 1 Sequence (3 emails over 7 days)**
- Email 1 (Day 1): Personalized thank-you referencing their specific engagement (question asked, session attended). Include the promised resource or recap. Soft CTA: schedule a call or see a demo.
- Email 2 (Day 3): One relevant case study or customer story tied to the event topic. CTA: read the story, then book time.
- Email 3 (Day 7): Direct ask. Reference their engagement again. Offer a specific next step (free audit, strategy session, pilot). Create mild urgency (limited spots, time-sensitive offer).

**Tier 2 Sequence (4 emails over 14 days)**
- Email 1 (Day 1): Thank-you with full event recap and key takeaways. No hard CTA yet.
- Email 2 (Day 4): Deliver one high-value asset (guide, checklist, framework) relevant to the event topic.
- Email 3 (Day 8): Share a short video or blog post that addresses a common pain point from the event. Soft CTA: reply with their top challenge.
- Email 4 (Day 14): Invite to a follow-on event (office hours, live Q&A) or offer a 1:1 consult. Low-friction CTA.

**Tier 3 Sequence (4 emails over 21 days)**
- Email 1 (Day 2): "Sorry we missed you" — share the event recording and top 3 takeaways.
- Email 2 (Day 6): One educational piece tied to the event theme. No sales language.
- Email 3 (Day 12): Social proof or customer story. CTA: see if this sounds like their situation.
- Email 4 (Day 21): Break-up style re-engagement. Ask if the topic is still relevant; offer to remove them from outreach or connect them with a resource.

### Step 4: Define Content Assets Needed
List any supporting assets required per sequence:
- Event recap PDF or landing page
- Case study or customer story link
- Demo or booking link (Calendly, Chili Piper, etc.)
- Follow-on event registration link
- Gated or ungated downloadable asset
