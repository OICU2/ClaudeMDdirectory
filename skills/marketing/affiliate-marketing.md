# Affiliate Marketing Skill

This skill designs, launches, and optimizes affiliate programs — from commission structures and partner recruitment to tracking, payouts, and performance analysis.

## Affiliate Program Workflow

### 1. Define Program Structure
- Set commission model: flat fee per sale, percentage of revenue (typically 5–30%), recurring commissions, or tiered rates by volume
- Define cookie window (standard: 30–90 days)
- Set minimum payout threshold and payment schedule (net-30, net-60, or monthly)
- Determine approved traffic sources: content sites, email, social, PPC (specify if brand bidding is allowed)

### 2. Choose Tracking & Platform
- **Self-hosted**: Tapfiliate, Post Affiliate Pro, Refersion
- **Marketplace networks**: ShareASale, CJ Affiliate, Impact, PartnerStack (SaaS-focused), Rakuten
- **Simple/lightweight**: Rewardful (Stripe-integrated), FirstPromoter
- Select based on: budget, existing tech stack, desired partner reach, and reporting needs

### 3. Recruit Affiliates
- Identify partner tiers: micro-influencers (1K–50K followers), niche bloggers, coupon/deal sites, industry newsletters, complementary SaaS tools
- Write outreach email with: specific why-them reason, commission rate upfront, proof of product-market fit (conversion rate or EPC if available)
- List on affiliate directories: Affilorama, AffiliatePrograms.com, and relevant network marketplaces
- Create a public affiliate landing page with: commission details, cookie duration, creative assets, and application form

### 4. Provide Partner Assets
- Produce: banners (300×250, 728×90, 160×600), text links, email swipe copy, social media captions, product images
- Write a one-page affiliate guide covering: program rules, brand guidelines, prohibited promotions, and how to get paid
- Set up a partner portal or Notion page with all assets organized by campaign or product

### 5. Activate and Communicate
- Send welcome sequence: Day 1 (welcome + assets), Day 7 (first promo tip), Day 30 (performance check-in)
- Create monthly affiliate newsletter: new offers, seasonal campaigns, top performer spotlight, updated creatives
- Run limited-time SPIFs (sales performance incentive fees) or bonus tiers to drive activation

### 6. Track and Optimize
- Monitor KPIs: clicks, conversion rate, EPC (earnings per click), AOV (average order value), reversal rate, ROAS
- Identify top 20% of affiliates generating 80% of revenue — offer higher tiers, exclusive deals, or co-marketing
- Audit low performers at 60 days: replace or re-engage with tailored outreach
- A/B test landing pages and creatives to improve affiliate conversion rates

### 7. Handle Compliance and Fraud
- Require FTC disclosure language in affiliate agreements
- Monitor for: cookie stuffing, trademark bidding violations, fake traffic, self-referrals
- Set automatic fraud flags in tracking platform for abnormal click-to-sale ratios

## Output Format

Deliver the following based on the specific request:

**Program Launch Plan**: Structured document with commission model, platform recommendation, recruitment strategy, and 90-day activation timeline.

**Outreach Templates**: 3–5 personalized email templates for cold outreach to different affiliate tiers (blogger, influencer, tool/SaaS partner).

**Commission Structure Recommendation**: A table comparing 2–3 commission models with projected payout costs at varying sales volumes.

**Affiliate Brief**: One-page onboarding document covering program rules, assets, links, and contact info — ready to send to new partners.

**Performance Audit**: Ranked affiliate table by EPC and revenue contribution, with specific action recommendations (scale, re-engage, or remove) for each tier.

All outputs should be ready to use
