# Webinar Replay Repurposing Plan

Analyzes a webinar transcript, recording summary, or outline and produces a complete, structured plan for repurposing it into clips, pull quotes, blog posts, social content, and email sequences.

## Repurposing Workflow

### Step 1: Extract Core Assets
- Identify the 3–5 most insightful or actionable moments from the webinar
- Pull 8–12 direct quotes that stand alone without context
- Note any data points, statistics, or surprising claims mentioned
- Flag the single best "hook" moment (surprising claim, strong opinion, or compelling story)

### Step 2: Define Video Clips
- Recommend 3–5 short-form video clips (15–90 seconds each)
- For each clip specify: timestamp range (if available), topic label, recommended platform (TikTok, Instagram Reels, LinkedIn, YouTube Shorts), and a suggested caption
- Identify 1 longer highlight clip (3–5 minutes) suitable for YouTube or a replay landing page

### Step 3: Build the Blog Post Plan
- Outline a long-form blog post (1,200–1,800 words) that expands on the webinar's central argument
- Provide: suggested title, meta description, H2 section headers, and a call-to-action at the end
- Identify a second, shorter blog post (500–700 words) written as a "key takeaways" recap

### Step 4: Social Media Content
- Write 5 LinkedIn posts using pull quotes or insights, each with a hook line, body, and CTA
- Write 5 Twitter/X posts (280 characters max) from the strongest quotes or data points
- Suggest 3 carousel or infographic concepts based on list-style content from the webinar

### Step 5: Email Sequence
- Draft a 3-email nurture sequence:
  - Email 1: Replay announcement + top 3 takeaways (send day of or day after)
  - Email 2: Deep-dive on the single strongest insight + soft CTA (send day 3–4)
  - Email 3: "Last chance to watch" + bonus resource or offer (send day 6–7)
- Each email includes: subject line, preview text, body copy (150–200 words), and CTA button text

### Step 6: Repurposing Calendar
- Lay out a 2-week distribution schedule showing which asset publishes on which day and channel
- Prioritize highest-ROI assets first (email replay, LinkedIn, blog post)

## Output Format

Deliver a structured markdown document with clearly labeled sections:

1. **Asset Inventory** — bulleted list of all extracted quotes, data points, and key moments
2. **Video Clips Plan** — table with columns: Clip #, Topic, Timestamp/Moment, Platform, Caption
3. **Blog Post Outlines** — two separate outlines with titles, headers, and CTAs
4. **Social Media Posts** — numbered posts grouped by platform, ready to copy-paste
5. **Email Sequence** — three complete emails with all fields filled in
6. **2-Week Distribution Calendar** — day-by-day table with Asset, Platform, and Notes columns

Total output should be comprehensive enough to hand directly to a content coordinator. Use headers, tables, and bullet points throughout. Avoid vague recommendations — every item should be specific and actionable.
