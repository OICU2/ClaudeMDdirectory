# Community Engagement Content Calendar

Creates a structured week-by-week content and engagement calendar tailored to a community's growth stage, platform, and member behavior patterns.

## Information Gathering

Before building the calendar, collect:
1. **Platform** — Discord, Slack, Circle, Facebook Group, Reddit, forum, or other
2. **Community size and age** — number of members, months/years active
3. **Growth stage** — Seed (0–200), Growing (200–2000), Scaling (2000+), or Mature/Stagnant
4. **Primary goal** — member acquisition, activation, retention, or re-engagement
5. **Content capacity** — how many hours per week the team can dedicate
6. **Community theme/niche** — what the community is about
7. **Current pain points** — low posting, lurkers, member churn, no events, etc.

If details are missing, make reasonable assumptions and state them explicitly.

## Calendar Construction Rules

**Map content types to growth stage:**
- **Seed stage**: 70% founder-led conversation starters, 20% member spotlights, 10% resource drops
- **Growing stage**: 40% discussion prompts, 30% member-generated content nudges, 20% events/AMAs, 10% announcements
- **Scaling stage**: 30% structured programming (weekly series), 30% community challenges, 20% member spotlights, 20% events
- **Stagnant/Re-engagement**: 50% win/story requests, 30% direct outreach content, 20% re-introduction campaigns

**Apply engagement rhythm patterns:**
- Post highest-effort content Tuesday–Thursday (peak activity for most communities)
- Use Monday for the week's kickoff prompt or challenge launch
- Use Friday for wins, celebrations, or casual conversation
- Reserve weekends for automated or low-lift content only
- Space posts minimum 4 hours apart on the same day

**Content type definitions to use:**
- **Discussion Prompt**: Open question designed to get replies, not just reactions
- **Member Spotlight**: Feature a specific member's win, story, or contribution
- **Resource Drop**: Curated link, template, or tool with a 1–2 sentence context note
- **Event Post**: AMA, live session, workshop announcement with clear CTA
- **Challenge**: Multi-day or weekly participation mechanic with a defined prompt
- **Re-engagement Hook**: Direct question or poll targeting lurkers explicitly
- **Announcement**: Product, community, or partner news — kept to max 1 per week

## Output Format

Produce the following structure:

---

**Community:** [Name or description]
**Platform:** [Platform]
**Growth Stage:** [Stage]
**Calendar Duration:** 4 weeks
**Weekly Time Investment:** [X hours/week]
**Primary Goal:** [Goal]

---

### Week-by-Week Calendar

For each of the 4 weeks, output a table:

| Day | Content Type | Post Title / Prompt | Goal | Notes |
|-----|-------------|---------------------|------|-------|
| Monday | Discussion Prompt | "What's the one thing..." | Activation | Tag 2–3 members to seed replies |
| Tuesday | Member Spotlight | Feature: [member archetype] | Retention | DM member for approval first |
| ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |

Include 5–7 posts per week for low-capacity teams, 8–12 for medium, 13+ for high.

---

### Engagement Actions (Non-Post Tasks)

List 3–5 weekly moderator/manager actions beyond posting:
- e.g., "Reply to every comment within 2 hours on Monday and Tuesday"
- e.g., "DM 3 lurkers who joined in the last 30 days with a personal welcome"
