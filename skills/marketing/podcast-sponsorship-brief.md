# Podcast Sponsorship Brief

This skill generates complete podcast sponsorship outreach packages including audience analysis, ad placement recommendations, and projected ROI figures that sponsors or podcasters can use in real negotiations.

## Workflow

1. **Identify the perspective**: Determine if the user is a podcaster seeking sponsors or a brand seeking podcast placements. Adjust framing accordingly.

2. **Gather core inputs**: Extract or ask for:
   - Podcast name, niche, and episode cadence
   - Listener demographics (age, income, profession, geography if known)
   - Download/listener numbers (monthly or per episode)
   - Target sponsor category or specific brand being pitched
   - Budget range or sponsorship tiers desired

3. **Build the audience profile**: Describe the listener segment in concrete terms — psychographics, pain points, purchasing behavior, and why this audience is valuable to the sponsor category. Cite industry benchmarks where relevant (e.g., podcast listeners are 45% more likely to visit a sponsor's website).

4. **Define sponsorship packages**: Create 3 tiered options:
   - **Bronze (Episode Sponsor)**: Pre-roll or mid-roll mention, 30–60 seconds, single episode
   - **Silver (Campaign Sponsor)**: Mid-roll placement, 60–90 seconds, 4–8 episode run, social mention
   - **Gold (Presenting Sponsor)**: Pre-roll + mid-roll + post-roll, 8+ episodes, co-branded content, newsletter inclusion, host-read endorsement

5. **Calculate ROI projections**: Use standard podcast CPM benchmarks ($18–$50 CPM depending on niche) to show:
   - Estimated impressions per tier
   - Cost per thousand listeners (CPM)
   - Projected click-through rate (1–4% for host-read ads)
   - Estimated leads or conversions at industry-standard rates
   - Comparison to equivalent digital ad spend (Google/Meta CPM benchmarks)

6. **Write the outreach pitch**: Draft a 150–200 word cold outreach email or deck introduction that leads with audience value, states the opportunity clearly, and ends with a specific call to action.

7. **Add credibility elements**: Include social proof suggestions (notable past guests, press mentions, community size, listener testimonials) and any relevant engagement metrics (reviews, Patreon backers, community members).

## Output Format

Produce a structured brief with these clearly labeled sections:

**PODCAST SPONSORSHIP PACKAGE — [Podcast Name]**

- **Audience Overview** (3–5 bullet points on listener profile)
- **Why This Audience Converts** (2–3 sentences on purchase intent and niche authority)
- **Sponsorship Tiers** (table or labeled blocks: Bronze / Silver / Gold with deliverables and pricing)
- **ROI Projection Table** (columns: Tier | Est. Impressions | CPM | Est. Clicks | Est. Conversions | Total Investment)
- **Comparable Ad Spend** (1–2 sentences benchmarking against Google/Meta/display CPMs)
- **Outreach Email Draft** (ready-to-send, 150–200 words)
- **Next Steps** (3 bullet points: follow-up timeline, negotiation note, contract/media kit suggestion)

Total length: 600–900 words. Use tables for pricing and ROI data. Write in professional but direct marketing language.
