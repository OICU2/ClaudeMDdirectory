# Media Pitch Angle Generator

This skill generates targeted, journalist-ready pitch angles customized to specific beats, publications, and audience contexts, activating whenever someone needs to translate a product or announcement into compelling media narratives.

## Pitch Generation Workflow

1. **Gather core inputs** — Collect the product/announcement details, any known target publications or journalists, and the desired outcome (coverage type: news brief, feature, opinion, podcast, etc.). If not provided, ask for them before proceeding.

2. **Identify the news hook** — Determine what makes this timely: a launch date, trend alignment, data release, cultural moment, regulatory change, or competitive shift. Every angle must anchor to a concrete hook.

3. **Map to journalist beats** — Generate distinct angles for at least 3 different journalist types from this list based on what's relevant:
   - **Tech/product reporters**: Focus on what's new, how it works, and what it disrupts.
   - **Business/finance reporters**: Emphasize market size, revenue impact, funding, or competitive positioning.
   - **Lifestyle/consumer reporters**: Lead with user benefit, emotional resonance, or behavioral change.
   - **Trade/industry reporters**: Highlight sector-specific implications, compliance, or operational shifts.
   - **Data/trends reporters**: Surface statistics, research findings, or pattern-level insights.

4. **Craft each angle** — For each beat, write:
   - A one-line pitch subject line (under 60 characters, no buzzwords)
   - A 2–3 sentence pitch body that leads with why their readers care, not what the product does
   - One suggested exclusive or added-value offer (data, early access, exec interview, etc.)

5. **Flag weak angles** — If the product lacks a clear news hook or differentiator, explicitly say so and suggest what additional proof points or timing would strengthen the pitch before outreach.

## Output Format

Produce a structured pitch brief with the following sections:

**News Hook Summary** (1–2 sentences stating the core timely angle)

**Pitch Angles by Beat** — One block per journalist type, formatted as:

> **Beat: [Beat Name]**
> **Subject Line:** [Under 60 characters]
> **Pitch Body:** [2–3 sentences, written in pitch-ready prose]
> **Value Add:** [Specific exclusive or offer]

Minimum 3 beats, maximum 5. Each pitch body must be written as if sending directly to a journalist — no placeholders, no brackets except for names. End with a **Pitch Strength Assessment** (3–5 sentences) noting what's working, what's missing, and one recommended first outreach target based on fit.
