# Content CTA Placement Audit

This skill audits long-form content for call-to-action placement effectiveness, identifies missing CTAs in high-engagement zones, and delivers prioritized recommendations for optimal insertion points.

## Audit Workflow

1. **Map existing CTAs**: Scan the full piece and list every existing CTA — its exact text, location (intro, body section, conclusion), and type (subscribe, purchase, download, contact, social share).

2. **Identify high-engagement zones**: Flag these sections as prime CTA real estate regardless of whether a CTA currently exists there:
   - Opening hook (first 100–150 words)
   - After a compelling statistic or data point
   - After a solved problem or "aha moment"
   - End of each major H2 section
   - Just before the conclusion
   - Final paragraph / conclusion

3. **Score current CTA placement**: For each existing CTA, evaluate:
   - **Relevance**: Does the CTA match the reader's intent at that moment in the content?
   - **Momentum**: Does it interrupt flow or ride natural stopping points?
   - **Clarity**: Is the value proposition of the CTA explicit?
   - Rate each: Strong / Weak / Misplaced

4. **Identify gaps**: Cross-reference high-engagement zones against existing CTAs. Flag any zone with no CTA present.

5. **Generate insertion recommendations**: For each gap or misplaced CTA, produce a specific recommendation including:
   - Where exactly to insert or move it (reference the surrounding text or heading)
   - What type of CTA fits the reader's mindset at that point
   - A sample CTA line or phrase they can use or adapt

6. **Check CTA diversity**: Ensure not every CTA is identical in type or phrasing. If all CTAs are the same, flag this and suggest varied micro-commitments (e.g., a soft CTA like "save this for later" before a hard CTA like "start your free trial").

## Output Format

Produce a structured audit report with these sections:

**CTA Inventory**
A numbered list of all existing CTAs with: location, CTA text, type, and rating (Strong / Weak / Misplaced) plus one-line reasoning.

**High-Engagement Zone Coverage**
A table or checklist showing each high-engagement zone, whether a CTA is present (✅ / ❌), and a brief note on opportunity level.

**Gap & Misplacement Findings**
Bulleted list of specific problems found — reference the actual content section by heading or quote.

**Prioritized Recommendations**
Numbered list ordered by impact (highest first). Each recommendation includes:
- Location (exact heading or surrounding sentence reference)
- Recommended CTA type
- Sample CTA copy (1–2 sentences, ready to use)
- One-sentence rationale

**Quick Wins**
A 2–3 item shortlist of the single easiest changes that would have the most immediate impact on conversion.

Keep the audit direct and specific to the submitted content. Do not give generic advice — every recommendation must reference the actual text provided.
