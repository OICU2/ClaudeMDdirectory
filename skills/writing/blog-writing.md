# Blog Writing

Produces complete, publish-ready blog posts with strong hooks, structured body content, and clear calls to action, optimized for both reader engagement and organic search.

## Writing Workflow

1. **Identify the goal** — Determine the post's primary purpose: educate, persuade, rank for a keyword, build authority, or drive conversions. Ask if unclear.
2. **Define the audience** — Identify who the reader is, what they already know, and what problem they need solved.
3. **Choose a headline** — Write a headline using a proven formula: How-To, Numbered List, Question, or Bold Claim. Make it specific and benefit-driven.
4. **Write the hook (first 2–3 sentences)** — Open with a surprising stat, a relatable pain point, a bold statement, or a short story. Never open with "In today's world" or generic throat-clearing.
5. **Structure the body** — Use H2 and H3 subheadings every 200–300 words. Each section answers one question or covers one idea. Use short paragraphs (2–4 sentences max).
6. **Add depth** — Include at least one of: a real example, a statistic, a quote, a comparison, or a step-by-step breakdown. Avoid vague generalizations.
7. **Optimize for SEO naturally** — Weave the primary keyword into the title, first 100 words, one H2, and the conclusion. Do not stuff keywords.
8. **Write the conclusion** — Summarize the key takeaway in 2–3 sentences, then include a clear call to action (subscribe, comment, read next, try something).
9. **Review for readability** — Vary sentence length. Replace jargon with plain language. Cut filler words (very, just, really, in order to).

## Output Format

Deliver the complete blog post in this structure:

```
# [Headline]

[Hook paragraph — 2–3 sentences]

[Introduction — 1 short paragraph setting up what the post covers]

## [H2 Section Title]
[Body content with short paragraphs]

## [H2 Section Title]
[Body content — may include bullet lists, numbered steps, or examples]

## [H2 Section Title]
[Body content]

## [Optional: FAQ or Common Mistakes section]

## Conclusion
[2–3 sentence summary + call to action]
```

- **Length:** 600–1,800 words depending on topic complexity (specify if not given)
- **Tone:** Match the brand voice if provided; default to conversational but authoritative
- **Format:** Markdown with proper heading hierarchy (H1 title, H2 sections, H3 subsections)
- **Do not include:** Author bio, meta description, or image placeholders unless explicitly requested
