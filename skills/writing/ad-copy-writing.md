# Ad Copy Writing

This skill generates conversion-focused ad copy for paid channels, producing platform-correct formats with compelling headlines, descriptions, and calls to action tailored to the target audience and campaign goal.

## Ad Copy Workflow

1. **Identify the platform** — Confirm whether the copy is for Google Search, Google Display, Meta (Facebook/Instagram), LinkedIn, or multiple channels. Each has different character limits and conventions.
2. **Extract the core offer** — Identify the product or service, its single strongest benefit, and what differentiates it from competitors.
3. **Define the audience and intent** — Clarify who the ad targets (demographics, job title, interest) and what action they should take (buy, sign up, learn more, book a call).
4. **Apply platform-specific rules:**
   - **Google Search Ads:** 3 headlines (max 30 chars each), 2 descriptions (max 90 chars each). Lead with keyword-rich, benefit-focused headlines. Use urgency or social proof in descriptions.
   - **Meta Feed Ads:** Primary text (up to 125 chars for preview), headline (27 chars), description (27 chars). Hook in the first line. Speak conversationally. Use "you" language.
   - **Google Display / Meta Banner:** Single punchy headline (under 40 chars) + one CTA word or phrase (under 20 chars).
   - **LinkedIn Ads:** Introductory text (150 chars visible), headline (70 chars), description (100 chars). Professional tone, ROI-driven, audience-aware.
5. **Write 3 copy variations** — Vary the angle across: (a) benefit/outcome, (b) pain point/problem, (c) social proof or urgency.
6. **Include a CTA in every ad** — Use action verbs: Get, Start, Try, Book, Download, Save, See, Join.
7. **Flag any missing inputs** — If product benefits, target audience, or landing page context are unclear, ask before writing.

## Output Format

For each platform requested, produce the following structure:

---
**[Platform Name] — Variation 1: [Angle Label]**
- Headline 1: `[text]` ([X] chars)
- Headline 2: `[text]` ([X] chars)
- Headline 3: `[text]` ([X] chars) *(if applicable)*
- Description 1: `[text]` ([X] chars)
- Description 2: `[text]` ([X] chars) *(if applicable)*

**[Platform Name] — Variation 2: [Angle Label]**
*(same structure)*

**[Platform Name] — Variation 3: [Angle Label]**
*(same structure)*
---

- Always show character counts inline
- Flag any field that exceeds the platform limit with ⚠️
- If writing for multiple platforms, group by platform
- End with a one-line note on which variation is recommended and why
