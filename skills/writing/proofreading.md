# Proofreading

This skill performs a thorough line-by-line review of written content to identify and correct grammar, spelling, punctuation, style, and consistency issues before publication.

## Proofreading Workflow

1. **Read the full text first** — Understand the tone, audience, and intent before marking anything.
2. **Check spelling** — Flag misspelled words, typos, and incorrect homophones (their/there/they're, its/it's, etc.).
3. **Check grammar** — Identify subject-verb disagreement, incorrect tense shifts, dangling modifiers, and sentence fragments.
4. **Check punctuation** — Look for missing or extra commas, incorrect apostrophes, misused semicolons, and inconsistent quotation mark style.
5. **Check consistency** — Flag inconsistent capitalization, hyphenation, number formatting (e.g., "3" vs. "three"), and terminology used for the same concept.
6. **Check formatting** — Note inconsistent heading styles, spacing issues, or list formatting problems.
7. **Preserve the author's voice** — Do not rewrite sentences for style unless a sentence is grammatically broken. Suggest, do not impose.
8. **Group errors by type** — Organize findings so the author can address categories efficiently.

## Output Format

Produce a structured report with three sections:

**Summary**
One to two sentences stating the overall quality of the text and the volume of issues found (e.g., "The text is mostly clean with 4 spelling errors and 2 punctuation inconsistencies.").

**Issues Found**
A numbered list of specific errors. Each entry must include:
- Line or sentence reference (quote the relevant text)
- Error type (Spelling / Grammar / Punctuation / Consistency / Formatting)
- The problem explained in plain language
- Suggested correction in quotes

Example:
> 1. "The team have finished there report." — **Grammar + Spelling** — "have" should be "has" for singular collective noun; "there" should be "their." → "The team has finished their report."

**Corrected Version** (if text is under 500 words)
Provide the full corrected text below the issues list, with all fixes applied. For longer texts, offer to produce it on request.
