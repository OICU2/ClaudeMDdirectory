# Long-Form to Executive Summary Converter

Converts lengthy documents into tight, decision-focused executive summaries that give busy leaders exactly what they need to act — no more, no less.

## Conversion Workflow

1. **Scan for structure** — Identify the document type (research report, business case, article, audit, proposal) to determine which elements matter most to an executive audience.

2. **Extract the core** — Pull out: the central problem or opportunity, the most significant findings or data points (limit to 3–5), and any conclusions the author draws.

3. **Identify implications** — Determine what the findings mean for the organization, stakeholder group, or decision at hand. Translate technical or detailed language into business impact.

4. **Surface recommended actions** — If the source document contains recommendations, extract and sharpen them. If it does not, derive logical next steps based on the findings. Always frame actions as specific and ownable.

5. **Cut ruthlessly** — Remove methodology details, background history, hedging language, and anything that does not directly inform a decision. Executives read for decisions, not context.

6. **Write in active voice** — Lead with verbs and outcomes. Avoid passive constructions, jargon, and filler phrases like "it is important to note."

## Output Format

Produce the executive summary using this exact structure:

---

**Executive Summary: [Document Title or Topic]**

**Overview** *(2–3 sentences)*
State the subject, scope, and purpose of the original document in plain language.

**Key Findings**
- [Finding 1 — specific, quantified where possible]
- [Finding 2]
- [Finding 3]
- [Finding 4 — optional]
- [Finding 5 — optional]

**Implications**
*(2–4 sentences)* Explain what these findings mean for the business, team, or decision-maker. Connect findings to risk, opportunity, cost, or strategy.

**Recommended Actions**
1. [Action — who does what by when, if determinable]
2. [Action]
3. [Action — optional]

**Bottom Line** *(1 sentence)*
The single most important thing the reader should take away.

---

Target length: 200–350 words total. Adjust only if the source document is exceptionally complex. Never exceed 500 words.
