# Multi-Platform Bio Variant Builder

Produces a complete set of bio variants in different lengths and tones from a single source of information, ready for use across websites, books, podcasts, social profiles, and press materials.

## Workflow

1. **Gather source material** — Ask for or extract: full name, professional title(s), key credentials or degrees, notable works or projects, current role and organization, years of experience, location (optional), preferred tone (academic, conversational, formal, punchy), and any must-include facts or links.

2. **Identify target platforms** — If not specified, default to producing all six standard variants. If platforms are specified, prioritize those and note which others are included as a bonus.

3. **Establish tone baseline** — Determine whether the person writes in first or third person by preference or platform convention. Default to third person unless told otherwise. Match voice to field: casual for creative/podcasting, formal for academic/press.

4. **Write all variants** — Produce each variant in a single pass, ensuring:
   - Each bio stands alone (no cross-referencing other variants)
   - Key credential appears in every version
   - Personality or differentiator appears in at least the medium and long versions
   - No filler phrases like "passionate about" or "dedicated to" unless the person explicitly uses them

5. **Label and organize** — Present each variant under a clear header with the platform context and word count.

6. **Offer revision hook** — After delivery, prompt with one question: ask if the tone, emphasis, or any specific detail needs adjustment across all versions.

## Output Format

Deliver six clearly labeled bio variants in this order:

---

**Twitter/Social Profile Bio** (~160 characters)
[Single punchy line. Title + 1-2 credentials + optional personality hook.]

---

**Podcast Guest / Event Speaker Bio** (~50 words)
[Who they are, what they do, one notable thing. Warm, conversational tone.]

---

**Short Bio — Website / About Page** (~75–100 words)
[Professional identity, key work, current focus, one humanizing detail.]

---

**Book Jacket / Contributor Bio** (~100–150 words)
[Formal or semi-formal. Credentials, publications or projects, affiliation, location if relevant.]

---

**Press / Media Kit Bio** (~200 words)
[Full professional narrative. Lead with impact, include career arc, notable achievements, current role, contact hook or website.]

---

**Long-Form / Speaker Page Bio** (~300–350 words)
[Complete story arc: background, expertise development, major work, current projects, personal angle, forward-looking statement.]

---

After all variants, include a one-line note flagging any information gaps that would strengthen the bios if filled in (e.g., missing publication titles, unclear current affiliation).
