## Ranked Candidates

### 1. email-triage-chief-of-staff
**Description:** Triages inbox, surfaces action items, drafts replies, flags urgents.
**ROI:** Harper Reed documented 6+ hrs/week recovered. Gmail MCP already available.
**Evidence:** Published live workflow Dec 2025. Multiple Reddit threads confirming.
**Triggers:** "triage my inbox", "what needs my attention", "clear my email backlog"

### 2. competitor-intel-monitor
**Description:** Weekly competitor scan — pricing, reviews, features, positioning changes.
**ROI:** Replaces $150–200/month tools (~$0.30/week in API costs). Directly relevant to RepuVault category.
**Evidence:** Documented on multiple indie hacker blogs; r/solopreneur threads.
**Triggers:** "monitor competitors", "what changed with [competitor]", "competitive scan"

### 3. cold-outreach-personalizer
**Description:** Researches a prospect, writes a personalized cold email/DM with specific hooks.
**ROI:** Lead gen agencies booking 150+ meetings/month with this workflow.
**Evidence:** Multiple HN threads, r/entrepreneur documented case studies.
**Triggers:** "write outreach for", "personalize cold email", "research prospect"

### 4. invoice-and-expense-organizer
**Description:** Processes receipts/invoices, categorizes expenses, flags anomalies, exports summary.
**ROI:** 2–3 hrs/month saved on bookkeeping. Live skill on ComposioHQ and fastmcp.me.
**Evidence:** Intuit/Anthropic partnership announced spring 2026 for QuickBooks integration.
**Triggers:** "organize my expenses", "categorize receipts", "monthly bookkeeping summary"

### 5. client-proposal-generator
**Description:** Turns a meeting transcript or brief notes into a polished client proposal.
**ROI:** Shyft.ai published live transcript-to-proposal skill. Agencies report 3–4 hrs saved per proposal.
**Evidence:** Documented workflow, multiple agency reports.
**Triggers:** "write proposal for", "turn notes into proposal", "draft scope of work"

### 6. seo-content-pipeline
**Description:** Keyword research → outline → draft → meta tags in one session.
**ROI:** Joe Karlsson documented full pipeline; multiple solopreneurs report 5–8 hrs saved per article.
**Evidence:** Most active GitHub ecosystem of all candidates. Detailed blog post with numbers.
**Triggers:** "write SEO article about", "content pipeline for", "rank for keyword"

### 7. paid-ads-creative-factory
**Description:** Generates multiple ad copy variants (headlines, body, CTA) for Meta/Google from a brief.
**ROI:** Solo founder documented 75% time savings, hundreds of variants end-to-end.
**Evidence:** Documented Meta ads pipeline on r/PPC and indie hacker communities.
**Triggers:** "write ad copy for", "generate ad variants", "Meta ads for my product"

### 8. weekly-business-pulse
**Description:** Pulls key metrics (revenue, churn, signups, expenses), surfaces anomalies, gives weekly summary.
**ROI:** Catching one anomaly a week earlier protects MRR. Stripe + Supabase already in stack.
**Evidence:** Documented on Stripe developer community, multiple SaaS founder posts.
**Triggers:** "weekly business summary", "how's the business doing", "pull my metrics"

### 9. daily-planning-briefing
**Description:** Reads calendar + email, outputs a prioritized daily plan with context on each item.
**ROI:** Morning triage dropped from 45 min to 5 min (Scrum.org case study). Calendar + Gmail MCPs available.
**Evidence:** Documented by multiple solopreneurs on Twitter/X, r/productivity.
**Triggers:** "plan my day", "morning briefing", "what's on my plate today"
