# Emerging Technology Analysis

This skill evaluates new and emerging technologies across maturity, adoption, risk, and business impact dimensions to help teams make informed adoption decisions.

## Analysis Framework

1. **Identify the technology scope**: Clarify the specific technology, version, or implementation pattern being evaluated. Distinguish it from related or predecessor technologies.

2. **Assess Technology Maturity (TRL/Gartner Hype Cycle)**:
   - Assign a Technology Readiness Level (1–9) with justification
   - Place on Hype Cycle: Innovation Trigger, Peak of Inflated Expectations, Trough of Disillusionment, Slope of Enlightenment, or Plateau of Productivity
   - Note age of the technology and how quickly it has progressed

3. **Map Adoption Trajectory**:
   - Identify current adopter category: Innovators, Early Adopters, Early Majority, Late Majority
   - List 3–5 real organizations already using it in production
   - Estimate time to mainstream adoption (months/years) with rationale
   - Flag any adoption blockers: regulatory, tooling gaps, talent scarcity, cost

4. **Evaluate Business Impact**:
   - Identify which business functions or processes this technology disrupts or improves
   - Quantify potential impact where possible (cost reduction %, speed improvement, new capability unlocked)
   - Identify industries where impact is highest vs. lowest
   - Note any competitive advantage window before commoditization

5. **Assess Risks**:
   - Technical risks: instability, vendor lock-in, breaking changes, lack of standards
   - Organizational risks: skill gaps, change management burden, integration complexity
   - Strategic risks: technology abandonment, regulatory uncertainty, security exposure

6. **Produce Adoption Recommendation**:
   - **Watch**: Too early, monitor developments
   - **Experiment**: Low-risk pilots appropriate now
   - **Adopt**: Ready for production use in right contexts
   - **Avoid**: Declining, superseded, or too risky
   - Include specific conditions that would change the recommendation

## Output Format

Produce a structured analysis with the following sections:

**Technology:** [Name + one-line definition]

**Maturity Snapshot**
- TRL Level: X/9 — [one sentence justification]
- Hype Cycle Stage: [stage] — [one sentence justification]
- Production-ready: Yes / No / Context-dependent

**Adoption Trajectory**
- Current adopters: [3–5 named organizations]
- Adopter category: [Innovators / Early Adopters / Early Majority]
- Mainstream ETA: [timeframe + rationale]
- Key blockers: [bullet list, max 3]

**Business Impact**
- Primary use cases: [bullet list of 3–5]
- Highest-impact industries: [list]
- Potential value: [quantified where possible]
- Competitive window: [how long before commoditized]

**Risk Register**
| Risk | Severity (H/M/L) | Mitigation |
|------|-----------------|------------|
| [risk] | [H/M/L] | [action] |

**Recommendation: [WATCH / EXPERIMENT / ADOPT / AVOID]**
[2–3 sentences explaining the recommendation and the specific conditions or team profiles for which it applies]

Length: 400–700 words. Use tables and bullets. Avoid vague qualifiers like "may" or "could" — make direct assessments with stated assumptions.
