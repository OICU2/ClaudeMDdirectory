# Research Question Operationalization Audit

This skill audits a research question for abstract or unmeasurable constructs and produces concrete operational definitions and measurable indicators for each flagged term.

## Audit Workflow

1. **Parse the research question** — Extract every noun, adjective, and verb phrase that represents a construct, concept, or phenomenon being studied.

2. **Flag problematic constructs** — Identify terms that are:
   - Abstract (e.g., "wellbeing," "success," "quality")
   - Multidimensional without specification (e.g., "engagement," "performance")
   - Relational but undefined (e.g., "impact," "influence," "relationship")
   - Time-sensitive without anchoring (e.g., "change," "improvement")

3. **For each flagged construct, produce:**
   - **Why it's problematic** — one sentence explaining the measurement gap
   - **Operational definition** — a precise, bounded definition specifying what counts and what doesn't
   - **At least 2 measurable indicators** — concrete data points, instruments, scales, or observable behaviors that capture the construct
   - **Level of measurement** — nominal, ordinal, interval, or ratio

4. **Assess the revised research question** — After operationalizing all constructs, restate the research question with flagged terms replaced by their operational equivalents. Check whether the revised question is testable, falsifiable, and scoped appropriately.

5. **Flag residual issues** — Note any remaining ambiguities, confounds, or constructs that require domain-specific expert input before they can be operationalized.

## Output Format

Produce a structured audit with the following sections:

---

**Original Research Question:**
[Quote the question verbatim]

**Flagged Constructs:**

| Construct | Problem | Operational Definition | Measurable Indicators | Level of Measurement |
|-----------|---------|----------------------|----------------------|----------------------|
| [term] | [1-sentence issue] | [precise definition] | [indicator 1; indicator 2] | [level] |

*(Repeat a row for each flagged construct)*

**Revised Research Question:**
[Restate the question using operational terms]

**Testability Assessment:**
[2-3 sentences on whether the revised question is empirically testable and what study design it implies]

**Residual Ambiguities:**
[Bulleted list of any constructs or issues that still need resolution, or "None" if fully resolved]

---

Length: concise — flag only genuine measurement problems, not stylistic preferences. Do not operationalize terms that are already precise and measurable.
